DAMAC Hills 2

Formerly known as Akoya “Damac Hills 2” is a master community by Damac Properties designed to offer a new type of luxury in Dubai. This water-themed community comes to present a truly rare lifestyle with a mix of villas, apartments and hospitality. Homes are all fully furnished and have access to world-class amenities. With a perfectly designed mixture of nature, wellness, amenities and features, this is the destination in Dubai where a dream home waiting for you.
