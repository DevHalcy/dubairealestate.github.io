Emirates Living

Emirates Living community in Dubai, even as this is one of the first largest communities in the middle east but known for its family-friendly & multi-ethnic setting. Currently comprises 5 residential developments (sub-communities) of villas & townhouses as Emirates Hills, The Springs, The Meadows, The Lakes and Hattan. This community was planned & developed by Emaar and is now also maintained by Emaar, so everything here is perfect & top of the class. Dedicated zones for play, laughing and eating together strengthen the sense of community living. Come and be a part of it and enjoy a better life, that your family deserve.

Master Plan of Emirates Living

Master Plan Details

Dubai Emirates Hills community is a best choice for families seeking for a better environment and high accessibility to everything for an easy peasy lifestyle. So for an ultra lavish living experience, get your own villa and live in the best mode.
