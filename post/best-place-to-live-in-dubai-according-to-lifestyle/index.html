Best Place To Live in Dubai, According To Lifestyle

Being recognized as the World’s safest city and remarkably presenting a diversity of lifestyles, Dubai is gaining huge popularity. People across the globe come to explore once to experience the lifestyle the city holds. Let’s have a look at some ideal areas and their lifestyles in Dubai.

Dubai – “World’s Top Lifestyle Destination”

That’s true! Why do I quote this? Although ,it is needless to explain about it still I feel like sharing what I feel about my experience. Because lots of people explore this city and everyone has their own experience, so do I. 
 
I don’t know from where do I start because there are millions of things running in my head. I was so impressed with Dubai and the discovery was just out of my mind I’d never thought that this city could be so beautiful that is beyond my imagination. 
  
Because Dubai holds much more and it could not possible to express a city in writing. Perhaps that’s why people do vlogs!  Anyway, like other tourists, I also visited this gold city, but with a different purpose. Like most people go to explore the wonders of Dubai, to see major attractions, its lifestyle and explore the EXPO which was held from 1st Oct 21- 31st March 22nd which I missed. 
 
I was there to discover the places where I can settle myself, yes, I wanted to relocate to Dubai and I wanted to know which was the best location to purchase a home there. I wanted to get all facilities close to me so that I do not need to use much effort to reach any place. Safety, conveniences, luxury and amenities were my first priority and for that, I visited and analysed some of the areas about which I had already heard before but wanted to see it personally. You can’t trust others’words until you see them with your own eyes. 
 
So, here I have brought a few collections of ideal areas according to lifestyle in Dubai. Downtown Dubai

In my list, I have added Downtown Dubai in the first place after a day of discovery for its advantageous location. It’s a family-friendly (pet-friendly too) community which is quite famous for its luxury lifestyle. Downtown Dubai is a prime location as it offers close proximity to some prominent business hubs like Business Bay, Dubai Design District and DFIC.

Major Attractions 

  • Burj Khalifa 
  • Dubai Fountain
  • Dubai Opera 
  • The Dubai Mall 
  • Dubai Aquarium 
  • Underwater Zoo
  • VR Park 

Properties Available

Downtown Dubai is a home of luxury apartments with the ultimate metropolitan lifestyle. Here you can get 1-6 bed apartments with state-of-the-art amenities. Given Below are the best luxury residential projects which had defined lavishness in a different way.

  • DAMAC Elegance Tower
  • Burj Crown 
  • District 11 Opal Gardens
  • Emaar Act One Act Two 
  • Emaar St. Regis Residence

Dubai Marina

Dubai Marina by Emaar is quite popular amongst ex-pats and investors and is home to 45,000 residents. The community is comprised of residential towers, service residences, hotels, malls, schools, and health care. It is known for its chic lifestyle and offers various things to explore. Living in Dubai Marina means enough bucks in your hand, though the place is quite expensive. The place is best known for offering a water-front lifestyle along with this it is quite safe for pedestrian life also. 

 Major Attractions 

  • Dubai Marina Mall 
  • JBR Beach 
  • Jumeirah Lake Towers
  • Palm Jumeirah 
  • Dubai Marina Yachts Club 

Properties Available in Dubai Marina 

Generally, you will get 1-3 bed apartments and penthouses in Dubai Marina. Check out the most desired projects below.

  • Emaar Marina Shores 
  • Emaar 42 52

Palm Jumeirah

The most iconic community in Dubai, best known for its waterfront lifestyle. This artificial island consists of private beaches, plenty of shopping, as well as dining options there are many state-of-the-art amenities planned in it. 

 Major Attractions 

  • The Aquaventure Waterpark 
  • The Sea Lion point 
  • Dolphin Bay 
  • The Lost Chamber 

Properties Available

Palm Jumeirah itself is a luxury destination and caught attention all over the world. The site is developed with villa, townhouses, apartments and hotels so choose the options according to your wish. Here you will get 1-4 bed residences in the given below projects. 

  • Nakheel Palm Beach Towers 2
  • Nakheel Palm Gateway
  • Nakheel Palm Beach Towers 3

Al Furjan

Now come to another address, Al Furjan – which offers apartments, villas, townhouses and studios.  The address is located between Sheikh Zayed Road and Shiekh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road. Also, the lifestyle In Al Furjan is safe. 

Major Attractions 

  • Battuta Mall 
  • The Gardens 

Properties Available

ARABIAN RANCHES

Arabian Ranches is a premium community featured with several lifestyle options. It’s a golf community and the most popular to-own villas and townhouses in Dubai. The community is a family-centric and pet-friendly neighborhood. Besides this, the community is featured with lush green, tranquillity, landscaped, tennis court and BBQ areas and maximum open space. You won’t feel like living in a concrete jungle.

Major Attractions 

  • JBR
  • Dubai Marina 
  • The JBR Beach 
  • Dubai Polo & Equestrian Club 

Properties Available

At Arabian Ranches, you will get a range of property options in different sub-community.  If you like living in townhouses then 2-4 bed options you’ll get. But if you are quite more demanding with space like me then you can go for a villa that comes in  2-7 beds at these sub-communities.

  • Saheel
  • Al Reem
  • Alvorada 
  • Mirador 
  • Palmera
  • Savannah

JUMEIRAH

This is another fine address, if you like to live close to the beach then this will be the perfect address for you. The serene, tranquil and relaxed environment of this address is so mesmerizing that when you approach here you will think as if all these are purposely done for you only. 

Family-friendly lifestyle, amenities around, and quick access to all civic amenities like schools, health care facilities and entertainment points developed close by make life better at this address. 

Major Attraction

  • Safa Park
  • Jumeirah Beach Park 
  • Jumeirah Open Beach 

AL BARARI

Chasing the true essence of luxury? Al Barari – spanned over 18 million Sq.ft this magnificent address with well-equipped world-class amenities within is just a treat for the end-user. Another family-friendly community featured villas and large-scale apartments in Dubai. Let you enjoy complete privacy by allocating private residences, yes, I am talking about villa residences. 

Major Attraction

  • Jumeriah Public Beach 
  • Kite Beach 
  • Umm Suqeim Beach 
  • Dubai Miracle Garden
  • Dubai Auto Drome
  • Dubai Polo Equestrian Club- most popular for horse riding and playing polo
  • IMG Worlds of Adventure – Thrill-packed themed park 

Properties Available 

At Al Barari residents will enjoy a fine living amongst the range of options available.  You will get 1-3 bed apartments as well as 4-7 bed villa residences for a completely independent life. Some of the most desiring sub-communities are mentioned below where you can find your dream home.
