FAQs

Q- What currency is best to use in Dubai?

Ans. Dirham is the all time currency and US dollar is accepted in some places of Dubai.

Q- Is Gold cheaper in Dubai?

Ans. Yes, gold is cheaper here and people come to buy pure close with no tax on it, and also one can get a wide range of options- Best place to buy gold (Dubai Gold Souk).

Q- Are watches cheaper in Dubai?

Ans. Second hand watches are much in trend and also if you go for top brand like Rolex then you get 20-25% off.  So all in all, Dubai is an ideal place to buy a watch because of its attractive range.

Q- Is Shopping in Dubai tax free?

Ans. Yes, it gives you tax free shopping experience where you can save a lot and buy more things.

Q- What can I buy in Dubai Duty Free?

Ans. There are lots of things you can from Dubai Airport- Arabic sweets, dry fruits, chocolate, perfumes, olives, liquor, cigarettes and more options to buy from Duty free shops.

Q- What is famous in Dubai for purchase?

Ans. Dubai is mainly for gold and duplicate items, so as a buyer you can get heaps of shopping options.

Q- What is the most popular market in Dubai?

Ans. There are 7 best markets in Dubai:- The gold souk, Textile souk, Flea market, Global village, Arte, the markers’ market, The Dubai mall and Ripe market.

Q- What is the best time to shop in Dubai?

Ans. November to March is considered as the best time because of the pleasant environment and Dubai Shopping Festival (DSF) is the main experience.

Q- How many days are enough for Dubai?

Ans. 5 days are enough to explore and to shop in Dubai, so if you are planning, then plan accordingly.
