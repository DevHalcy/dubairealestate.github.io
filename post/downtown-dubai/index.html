Why Should You Live In Downtown Dubai?

If you are looking for the best real estate investment opportunity then Downtown Dubai is the perfect place, know why?

What comes to your mind with the name “Dubai Downtown”?

Burj Khalifa?

Dubai Fountain?

Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo?

But all these attractions are only related to the tour & travel industry.

Think again as a real estate investor or home seeker in Dubai Downtown.

Now high-rise luxury apartments, penthouses and villas/townhouses with eye-catching views of the waterfront, extreme luxury & 5-star hospitality and so many things are reflected in your mind. Here you can find almost every global brand in apparel, gadgets, lifestyle and f&b. As well as shoppers from different parts of the world coming here to admire the luxury and beauty of these shopping malls. Some of the best popular malls & markets place in Dubai Downtown are:-

Comprises approx 1200 stores, an ice skating rink, a walk-through aquarium, a gaming zone and many other attractions and exciting experiences for the visitors. Offering parking spaces for more than 14,000 vehicles and many other conveniences. There are many other features & specifications of Dubai Mall that we cannot explain here, due to word limits.

So, Dubai Downtown is the place where you can enjoy a modern & traditional shopping experience.

Getting Around Is Easy (Modes of Transportation in Dubai Downtown)

Masterplan of Dubai city was designed with a wide network of roads, at that time most of the Dubai residents loved to travel by their luxury cars but now the scenario has completely changed. Now everyone in Dubai prefers public transport for travelling from one point to another. Dubai RTA’s (Roads and Transport Authority) public transport network comes one of the best & well-organized in the world. There are clean & safe buses, frequent metro service, a well-monitored private cab system and water taxis, everything here is well-planned & well-maintained.

Metro: If we talk about Metro in Downtown Dubai then it has Red Line connectivity and 5 stations (Burj Khalifa, Financial Centre, Business Bay, Emirates Towers and Al Jafiya). From any of these metro stations, you can easily connect with other lines and locations in Dubai. Operating hours are 7 AM to 11 PM but on Friday 10 AM to 11 PM.

Dubai Trams or Trolleys: Are operational since 2014 and are one of the best and most enjoyable means of transportation in Dubai Downtown.

Dubai Abra: if you want to enjoy the traditional way of transportation then this is the best option. Natives of Dubai or UAE are familiar with the word “Abra” but this word is new to the foreigners. “Abra” is a traditional small boat used to ferry people from one place to another. Now Dubai Ferry service comes under the Roads and Transport Authority of Dubai and consider one of the most reliable forms of transportation.

Everything mode of transportation in Dubai like the metro, buses, trams and water taxi etc. are efficiently managed by RTA and the best thing is that you don’t need to buy different tickets but a single NOL card.

Downtown Dubai Offers Outdoor Activities (Best Outdoor Activities To Enjoy In Dubai Downtown)

As you know, Downtown is the top prime location of Dubai, comprising the tallest skyscrapers in the world, the biggest malls, and many other attractive features. And this is also one of the best reasons or motivations for the home seekers to buy property in Dubai Downtown. Some of the best outdoor activities to enjoy in Downtown Dubai are:-

Observatory Decks: There are many high-rise towers in Downtown Dubai where you can enjoy picturesque views of the Dubai Skyline. Observatory decks in Burj Khalifa are world-famous for presenting 360-degree views of Dubai’s skyline. Here you can enjoy panoramic views from two observatory decks positioned on 124th and 125th floors.

Reel Cinema: Best place Downtown to enjoy movies with the best sound & elevated effects. Located inside the world-famous Dubai Mall on the 2nd floor, a big screen and THX-certified speakers of reel cinema enhance the experience of the viewers.

Dubai Fountain: The world-famous largest performing & choreographed fountain is also positioned in Downtown Dubai. We cannot describe the performance & views of this fountain in the words and seeing it once is not enough for you. This is a place that you want to visit again & again.

The Dubai Trolley: A perfect way to explore Downtown and this is also the best outdoor activity to enjoy in Dubai. Dubai Trolley is a tram service developed & maintained by Emaar. The trams are double-decker and open from the top, running on the 7 kilometres long loop track around Dubai Downtown. Your trip starts from MBR Boulevard, you get to explore many world-famous attractions and the best thing is that you can hop on hop off anywhere you want.

Visit Burj Park:  Burj Park is a man-made island alongside Dubai Fountain and accessible only through bridges. This is one of the best family hangout places Downtown as well as intended for parties, fashion shows and fairs. Views of Burj Khalifa & Dubai fountain make Burj Park a perfect hangout place.

Xline: A tour of Dubai Downtown is fun but if this tour through a zip line then nothing could be better than it, every it could add more fun & thrill. But this tour is not for the faint heart and if you are a thrill lover and want to explore an exciting side of Dubai then this is one of the best outdoor activities.
