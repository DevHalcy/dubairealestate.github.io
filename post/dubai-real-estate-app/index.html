Last and important, on ‘Map View’ you can have a perfect look on the list of properties as per the location you pre-select.

4. SMART INVESTMENT MAP (Available on apk extension)

One of the vital portals under Dubai Real Estate Market is designed and planned for the real estate professionals who can list down their properties for rent or sale, so all in all, it is a perfect platform where one can talk to the landlord directly and even the management companies and brokers are the best options.

And for more feasibility, the app Smart Investment Map gives you space to complete the sale transactions without visiting Dubai Land Department. So always stay connected to the investment opportunities which help you to make the right decision.

SALIENT FEATURES OF THE SMART INVESTMENT MAP APP

  • Direct contact with the landlord
  • Freedom to list your property 
  • Provides a list of properties for sale or rent
  • With market price trends to make comparisons 
  • Offers the list of nearby places to understand the location
  • Financial transactions processed through Noqodi- E-payment platform

APP GIVES AN ACCESS TO:-

  • Properties for sale
  • Properties for rent

RATING- 9.7

So if you want to check the properties which are available for rent or sale in Dubai, then you can install this app as one platform that brings you close to the top properties available in every budget fits into your pocket, and also major transactions are performed on the app itself where it saves a lot of time.

5. EJARI (Available on apk extension)

Excellent and valuable app for property management companies and property owners representatives where the Ejari app is planned for rental & lease management companies, and here with online access one can curtail the time involved in lease registration, and special features such as rental index calculator (RERA), cancellations, transfer and renewal makes it the lucrative app for the tenants and owners both.

SALIENT FEATURES OF EJARI APP

  • Calculate the rental 
  • Print Ejari certificate 
  • Register Ejari online
  • Pay Ejari fee (AED 170) through the app

APP GIVES AN ACCESS TO:-

  • Tenants to manage maintenance contracts
  • Draft rental contracts into legal binding documents 

Next time when you want to draft your documents or calculate the rental get the best use of Ejari app.

Note* It is a mandatory app for the landlords because if they don’t register then they have to face the penalty of Dh50,000 for non registration of the rental contracts on Ejari. Landlords hold the prime duty to register the Ejari and this can also be done by the tenant if needed. 

6. DUBAI BROKER APP (Available on IOS and Androids)

It is an appropriate app for the investors, real estate broker and agents as it displays the real time information, performance, number of transactions by the broker and more data to create trust between the user and the agent because such information leads to lucrative investment.

Also, the app offers ownership certificates without going anywhere physically to get it. In addition to this, real estate brokers will get E-card service which is a perfect replica of traditional card. And to control the manipulation the app creates a contract between the seller & buyer and further completes the process of ownership transfer of the property through the Registration Trustee offices, so major work is done with the help of the Dubai Broker App.

SALIENT FEATURES OF THE DUBAI BROKER APP

  • Gives an access to select qualified real estate broker
  • Avoid manipulations by listing down the verified brokers
  • Available on IOS and Androids
  • Access to ownership certificate verification service
  • Incorporated with Dubai Land Departments broker Real Estate License Management system (Trakheesi)

APP GIVES AN ACCESS TO:-

  • Find verified real estate agent
  • Real Estate Broker
  • Investors  

7. TAQYIMEE (Available on Apk extension)

Do you want to know the value of your property? Then Taqyimee is the smart real estate app in Dubai that connects to the third party evaluator which evaluates its right cost for you, and this would be trusted as the third party valuator is directly from a list of DLD approved valuator.

For its best usage if you are an investor seeking for a lucrative property, then this app will serve the best options along with the price list to sort the right investment option.

SALIENT FEATURES OF TAQYIMEE APP

  • Done right evaluation of your property
  • Evaluate all types of commercial and residential properties

APP GIVES AN ACCESS TO:-

  • Evaluate your property price
  • Investors to find the best property
