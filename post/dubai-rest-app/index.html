Dubai REST App - How To Register, Functionality & Usage

Dubai Rest App is a revolutionary application launched by DLD to go paperless and increase the convenience of landowners & tenants in Dubai Real Estate.

In 2018 Dubai Land Department has introduced an online platform the REST App (application) to solve the common real estate problems of the Dubai Inhabitants. If you are wondering what actually this application is, how it is work and what its uses are, then read this blog attentively. 

But before proceeding, here are a few things that you should know. The REST App serves significant real-estate stakeholders not only in Dubai but the entire UAE, thanks to smart & easy to navigate features. The main motive behind its launch is that the DLD wants to mark its position in the global race of smart cities.

How To Use The Dubai Rest App

You can download & install this app from Google Play Store or iOS. After downloading you need to create your account in Dubai Rest App by using the detail of UAE Pass. 

After logging in, you will need your Emirates ID for registering as a tenant or property owner. After submitting your Emirates ID, you have to wait for confirmation from DLD. After the confirmation, you will get access to all the details related to the properties.

Through this process, you can only log in or create your account. For profile updations, users are requested to visit the Property Trustee office or the DLD office. 

Key Features Of The Dubai Rest App

In a few words, this smart application can be very useful to perform several real estate-related tasks without visiting the DLD office. Some of the important key features of The Dubai Rest App are:- 

Real-Time Information

A best & smart feature of the Dubai Rest App is the option of real-time information. So, by using this app you can get full details of off-plan properties. You can track the construction status, get the actual images, track your payment status, outstanding amount and many other details. 

All of the above-mentioned details only support keeping a track of the development of the Off-Plan Properties In Dubai. As well as, data related to the sales index in Dubai and the property service charges index are also accessible on the REST app.

Real Estate Brokers

This smart application also works as a directory of registered real estate brokers in Dubai. Not only are the names & IDs of the real estate agents but you can also check their ratings, so you can choose the best and most reliable agent. As well as, you will have the access to important information about builders, sellers and management etc. 

Real Estate Services And E-Payments

Every real estate transaction and other services are also accessible through this app like leasing, renting & selling. Not only these services but you can also send applications, your concerned letter and apply for valuation certificates. Through this app, you can buy, sell or rent any apartment, villa or townhouse in Dubai. The fees for all these services can be paid through Noqodi (a certified payment portal in UAE).

Mortgages

Not only home buyers, sellers, property owners and tenants can use this app, but banks can also use this application for many services like transactions, providing mortgages, withdrawing mortgages and other services. 

Property Transactions

This app also works like a real estate wallet that allows its users to execute different types of transactions in real estate. A user can sell, buy, mortgage and even rent their unit anywhere in Dubai.

Other Services Offered By The Dubai Rest App

Other than the obvious, here are some of the key services provided by the Dubai Rest app. 

  • Verification & registration of title deed
  • Services catalogue
  • Status of off-plan developments with actual images
  • Different types of real estate transactions
  • Variety of certificate generations
  • Fee calculations

Conclusion

Dubai Rest App is providing a one-stop solution in real estate for property owners, tenants, real estate agents and people who are directly or indirectly linked with the real estate field. In a few words, this application brings the DLD office to you, no matter where you are.

FAQs

Q. How To Register An Ejari Contract in The Dubai Rest App?

Ans. The property owner and tenants can register their Ejari contract in the Dubai Rest app by following these steps -  Go to ‘Settings’ followed by ‘RERA’ select ‘Register Ejari Contract’ and fill in the mandatory details. Register An Ejari Contract is done. 

Q. Where Is The Rental Index Located In The Rest App?

Ans. You can easily the rental index in The Rest App under the RERA service options. The section is used to estimate the rent of property according to property type, total area and annual rent. 

Q. How To Verify Title Deed Through Rest App?

Ans. You just need to submit the year, property type, and name & certification number to variety the title deed in the Dubai Rest App. 

Q. What is Dubai Rest App used for?

Ans. Dubai Rest App is a completely digital system to manage real estate activities by owners, tenants, and other service providers.
