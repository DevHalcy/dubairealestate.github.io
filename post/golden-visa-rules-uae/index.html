What Is A Golden Visa In UAE?

GOLDEN VISA, introduced in 2019 came as a bestowal allowing the foreign nationals to live, work and study in the UAE. The visa is valid for 5-10 years and will be automatically renewed. It was already attractive to countless people since the time it was introduced, but becomes more striking with the recent changes announced by the Government of UAE.  Expats would be really happy knowing the quick-witted changes that took place in the Golden Visa Scheme.

What Are These Changes?

The UAE government announced the availability of different types of residency options available for the people traveling to the UAE with residency permits and new entry visas. 

UAE Government recently brought some amendments to the Golden Visa UAE easing the eligibility criteria and defining and expanding the categories of beneficiaries. The recent amendments allowed the Golden visa holders to sponsor/her family members and domestic help as well. 

But if you are applying for the Golden Visa without any sponsor then your investment has to be a minimum of Dh2 million. You don’t need to have that in cash form, it should be ascribed in the form of investment funds; or commercial or industrial investment with mentioned legal entities showing paid-up capital, the owner of the company who is paying a tax minimum of Dh250, 000 annually to the Federal Tax Authority.

Is everyone eligible for the Golden Visa?

No, heed down to know who are eligible for Golden Visa. 

Who Is Eligible For The Golden Visa?

Not everyone is eligible for the Golden Visa, Some are determined by the income, while some are classified according to UAE Cabinet Resolution; Investors, entrepreneurs, specialized talents including the doctors, scientists, artists, bright & scientific capabilities students from the exceptional schools & universities and researchers are eligible for the visa. But the eligibility aspects differ for each person like an investor should have no less than AED 2 million worth of public investment, specialized talents can apply for a 5 or 10-year visa, and students can get the golden visa on the basis of percentage and the university they hail from.

What Are The Requirements For The Golden Visa?

The different talents allowed for the Golden Visa should also fulfill the various requirements that are different for every group. Though requirements are different but sometimes it also depends on the individual faithfulness to the terms and conditions under which the Golden Visa was granted.

Know The Criteria For The Golden Visa

Golden Visa permits a 10-year residency in UAE and this is granted to the people who have made a significant contribution to the country’s growth. This growth can be related to anything be it arts, television, Cinema and theater, sports, poetry, and more. In 2019, the idea of providing a Golden Visa was approved and in April 2021, Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid, Vice President and Ruler of Dubai, approved the executive regulations on the creation of new types of visas and a Golden Visa residency system.

Golden Residence For Professionals:

  • Professionals category attracts different people and is quite expanded. It includes the highly skilled workers having proficient and preeminent qualifications and degrees.
  • They should possess professional experience from different fields including engineering, healthcare, IT, business & administration, law, and social science.
  • According to the Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation’s categorization, professionals need to have a valid UAE employment contract at the mentioned occupational level and with a minimum monthly income of AED 30,000.

Golden Residence For Real Estate Investors:

  • Real Estate Investors shouldn’t have a worth of public investment of less than AED 2 million; it is in both the form of the investment fund and a company.  
  • A minimum of 60% of the total investment must not be a real estate investment. whole investment should not be taken in the form of a loan and an individual should have ownership of everything.
  • This visa can be extended as well in the business if each partner is contributing AED 2 million. The executive director or an advisor can also apply for the Golden Visa without a sponsor.

Golden Residence For Entrepreneurs:

  • Under the set of new executive regulations announced by the UAE cabinet, entrepreneurs can apply for the Golden Visa.
  • They should be a partner in the company registered in the UAR under the SME category and should hold a revenue not less than AED 1 million.
  • Then the entrepreneurs who are a founder or partners in an entrepreneurial project that was sold at a minimum of AED 7 million can enjoy the UAE Golden Visa.

Golden Residence For Exceptional Talents:

  • There is a specific group of specialized talents that includes various categories like doctors, researchers, scientists, engineers, and artists.
  • They are also allowed for a 10-year visa if an individual will meet the criteria respective to their fields and departments. For children and spouses, this extends.
  • Coders come in this category irrespective of whether them being a UAE residents or not. If they are granted approval from the Office of Artificial Intelligence in the UAE Government or the Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship they can apply for the Golden Visa.

Golden Residence For Scientists: 

  • All the scientists and the researchers with brilliant brains having specific achievements and strong influence in their fields can be allowed for
  • Golden Visa on the recommendation of the Emirates Scientists Council.
  • An individual applying from the field of Scientists and professionals should possess a Ph.D. or master’s degree in specific science disciplines including engineering, technology, life science, and more; from the leading universities around the corner of the world. Some significant research achievements can be an add-on.

Golden Residence For Outstanding Students and Graduates:

  • Students who passed out from brilliant colleges and universities can apply for the Golden Visa having an outstanding rank & percentage, if he/she is having some achievements in academics that can be a cherry on the top.
  • Having graduation from software & hardware engineering, computer science, IT, AI, data science, big data, and electrical engineering with a good percentile gives a plus point in the list of outstanding students.

Golden Residence For Humanitarian Pioneers:

  • UAE has always been impactful with its humanitarian initiatives and their empowered works and it can become a second home to these individuals.
  • UAE always extends its commitment to the humanitarian sector pioneers and specialists. This was announced to make the mark for International Humanitarian Day.

Golden Residence For Frontline Heroes: 

  • All the healthcare professionals that have fought the deadliest pandemic can be granted golden visas by the United Arab Emirates.
  • The decision has been made by The Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan. In fact, special consideration will be given to the individuals who made extraordinary efforts and sacrifices.
  • This application will be viewed by the Government departments dealing with long-term stays.

Getting a golden visa is a dream of everyone and those who have earned it enjoy several benefits. Let’s look at some primary and then the detailed benefits.

Some More To Avail…

Visa is an imperative part required for anyone to legally enter another country. But the process is time-consuming and tiring, and after the whole process if the immigration officials deny the application then it is a whole disaster. But if you are a golden visa holder then the applicants can enjoy the free services and settle comfortably in the region.

Several Discounts:

In Abu Dhabi, the Golden Visa holders can enjoy several eye-catching discounts. Be it car, property, health, hospitality, and other services, various incentives can be enjoyed by the golden visa holders. In fact, several discounts can be availed on the properties from developer Imkan Properties and banks offer reduced mortgage fixed-interest rates as well.

Special Treatment:

Golden Visa holders enjoy some special discounts on their leisure and pleasure. They get special discounts on the dining, spa, gym, stays, and more at some of the selected hospitality destinations in Abu Dhabi. You can sit, chill and relax with your loved ones at a special price.

Security Purpose:

The main benefit of having a golden visa is security. There is no doubt that the UAE government aims to provide expatriates, investors, and anyone else considering making the UAE their home with an extra reason to feel secure about the future. The Government is taking every necessary step for it.

FAQs

1. Does your golden visa get nullified if you stay outside UAE?

Golden Visa allows you to stay for 10 years and even if you are not staying in UAE then also your visa does not get nullified.

2. Can a Golden Visa holder sponsor his family?

Golden Visa holders can sponsor not only the family members but dependents and the senior employees as well. Through this sponsorship varies from each category.

3. What is the cost of a Dubai Golden Visa?

A five-year UAE Golden Visa will cost you AED 650. AED 150 will be charged for application and AED 500 for issuance. The 10-year Golden Visa UAE will cost you AED 1,150.

4. Can a doctor get a Golden Visa?

Doctors need to meet the specific criteria to get the visa like a Ph.D. degree from a top-notch university, any award or appreciation certificate, major research work, any published book or article from a respective field, etc. are needed.
