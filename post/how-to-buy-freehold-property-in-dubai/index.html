What If I Ask You About A Freehold Property? Do You Know?

So freehold property is the one which is ‘free from hold’ it is just the owner that has 100% ownership and a freehold property will not require any consent when you plan to sell out.

Some Basics You Should Know About Freehold Property:-

  • The buyer becomes 100% owner
  • Enjoy 100% right on your property 
  • The cost of freehold property is a little higher
  • No tenure in freehold property
  • There is no such duty to follow 
  • Easy to get a bank loan
  • Do not involve any charges

After getting a complete definition on a Freehold Property next let us take you the standard process of buying a property in Dubai.  

How To Buy Freehold Property In Dubai?

Let us give you the basics of Steps of buying freehold property in Dubai

  • Formulate a contract between the buyer and seller
  • Signing the agreement for sale
  • Application for a no objection Certificate
  • Ownership transfer with DLD

After completing the above legality, a new title deed will be issued in your name and finally, you will become a property owner in Dubai and start having its advantages.

Advantages Of Owning A Freehold Property In Dubai

  • You can modify your home either interior or exterior with any consent 
  • You have right to rent out your home
  • Leeway to install anything in your home 
  • Keep pets if you wish
  • No charges to be paid like ground rent

So with every freedom the freehold property you can have the best use of your property where the complete ownership doesn’t involve anyone else and you have absolute rights to its safety, changes and construction.

After this, if you are still confused with leasehold, then let us give you the basic difference between both of them.

Freehold VS Leasehold Dubai

FREEHOLD LEASEHOLD
You are a complete owner You are not the 100% owner
Higher Resale Value 30 Years Old Leasehold Property May Depreciate
Tenure Is Forever Lease Is Generally For 30, 60 and 99 years
Little expensive Relatively cheaper
No Additional Charges Rent or Extra Charges To Be Paid
No Need To Renew Any Paper Need To Renew The Lease
