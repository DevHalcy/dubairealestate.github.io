4. Tax Free Regime

So apart from the next level lifestyle, the reality that makes the right connection is being a tax free city where there is no income tax on the income generated here. So as far as the investment is concerned, there is no property tax on homes as well as no income or inheritance tax.

However, when the property changes hand 4 per cent transfer tax is imposed, and the fee is divided between both the seller & buyer. Also, the owner and tenants are expected to pay a municipality tax which comes out to be 5% of their home’s annual rental income and this is to fund the local services for better growth and stability for all.
