Real estate investors planning to buy a property here can expect an average gross yield of 7.20% for 1 Bed Apartment. Many Amenities Something For Everyone

Normally in every family, different members have different hobbies or choices for recreation. The best thing is that DAMAC Hills is one of the few communities in Dubai where every member of your family will find something interesting for recreation. Ranging from a grand clubhouse to many outdoor avenues for recreation, this community has everything to keep you engaged the whole day. Some of the best options are:

Water Town

With many best options for water sports & activity lovers, here you can swim or just float on the water of a lazy river, soak in the sun rays by Malibu Beach in winter or enjoy the company of the nearest in dearest while boating or in the cafes.

For the small kids, there is a kids pool, splash pad, slides, and many other best & safe options under the care of experts. 

Sports Town

A heaven for the sports person who always likes to enjoy an active & energetic life. This  part of DAMAC Hills 2 is ready with an outdoor gym, football ground, cricket pitch and nets, table tennis, tennis, and many other full of energy sports

Downtown

A perfect place to take a break from your daily routine and celebrate the best outdoor living with good energy and spend quality time. Recharge yourself with Yoga for the entire day at the zen garden, take an easy stroll on the velvety green grass and enjoy a family BBQ as dinner or lunch.

Motor Town

As its name suggest, a perfect place for moto-race lovers. Enjoy the Go-karting within the peripheries of the DAMAC Hills 2 community, because Motor Town comprises an adrenalin-inducing racing circuit of its own.

Equestrian Town

A perfect outdoor option is DAMAC Hills 2 and this is a place that everyone wants to visit more frequently. Here you can start your day by shopping for fresh farming & dairy products and enjoy peaceful & green surroundings.
