Is Dubai South a Good investment?

Dubai South is an extraordinary location in Dubai with extraordinary development prospect for residential & commercial developments. Read this blog and explore the best investment opportunities.

About Dubai South

Previously known as the Dubai World Central and now world-famous as Dubai South. This region is now getting ready to be an independent district with its eco-system comprising logistics, aerospace hub, and commercial & residential developments. This region is spread over 145 sq. km., and a major part of the total land lies in the world’s greatest airport “Al Maktoum International Airport” spread over 14,000 Hectares.

Dubai South Area

Dubai South is planned as an integrated city within Dubai peripheries, creating an ecosystem to live, work, enjoy and invest. To achieve this, the entire Dubai South area is divided into 8 zones:

  • Commercial District
  • Residential City
  • Golf City
  • Exhibition District (Expo 2020 site)
  • Business Park (Freezone)
  • Logistics District
  • Humanitarian District
  • Aviation District (Home to Al Maktoum International Airport)

Real estate investors & home seekers can buy freehold & leasehold properties such as apartments, penthouses, villas & townhouses. As well as, lots of opportunities are here for setting up businesses. World-class infrastructure, modern transportation system, high-end facilities & amenities makes Dubai South the biggest master-planned development in UAE. 

Dubai South Residential City

This dedicated zone is cover 715 hectares positioned in the North-Eastern part of Dubai South which offers the best housing options at an affordable price (comparatively other locations of Dubai). There was three biggest motive behind the planning of Dubai South Residential City.

  • Living close to the workplace
  • Meet the housing demand from the people who are working in other districts of Dubai South
  • Provide affordability & compatibility to a specific group.

This district can house approx 1 Lacs people and employ 20 thousand people. Positioned next to the peripheral road to provide access to other districts of Dubai South. As well as, a dedicated area in the South-Eastern part of the residential city for blue-collar professionals.

Dubai South Free Zone Master Plan

Aviation District

Spread over 6.7 sq. km. completely dedicated to the aviation sector. The zone is also known for Dubai Airshow, the Middle East Business Aviation Show and as VIP Terminal.

Logistics District

This district is especially meant for companies that are affiliated with the supply chain. The zone offers custom-made as well as ready-to-use services for logistic companies, freight forwarders and big freight carriers. Ease of access to the Al Maktoum International Airport for air cargo services.

Business Park

As the name defined the district is specially made for office spaces, new startups, and MNCs of different sectors. The business park also comprises a business centre that offers an all-inclusive office solution 24/7.

Business Facilities in Dubai South

  • 100% foreign ownership in Dubai South Free Zone
  • No personal income, or corporate taxes for Dubai South Free Zone
  • Dynamic international community
  • Full repatriation of Capital and profits
  • Strategic location boosting opportunities 
  • Leasing options
  • The simple and quick set-up process
  • World-class infrastructure
  • Exemption from all import and export duties; however, you can't import into mainland

Types of Licenses Available in Dubai South Free Zone

There are 6 types of licences are available in Dubai South Zone.

  • Logistics License - for specified logistics services
  • Industrial License - for specified light manufacturing activities
  • General Trading License -   for import, export, store items and sell products in the UAE market through a local distributor
  • Service License  - for services specified in the License within the Dubai South Free Zone and elsewhere in the UAE
  • Education License -  for educational and social services, educational and training, and educational consultancy services
  • Commercial License - for specified commercial activities out of the UAE, or in the UAE through the local distributor

Why Dubai South?

Till now we have described different districts & some facts about Dubai South. But, still, there is a question in your mind - why invest in Dubai South? There are countless reasons to invest here, but we are going to describe some of the best here.

Tremendous Growth Potential

As you know that the masterplan of Dubai South has every ingredient that is required for the tremendous growth of any region. So, expectations from the city region are very high. How we can forget to mention the name behind this outstanding master planning - His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum and his planning of Dubai Plan 2021

Wide Range Of Properties

The residential district in Dubai South is positioned adjacent to the Expo 2020 site and spread over 6.7 million sq. km. This district itself is divided into 4 zones.  

  • The villages- comprise Villas, Townhouses and Apartments
  • Parklane - comprises studios, Apartments and Townhouses 
  • The Pulse - Apartments & Townhouses
  • Sakany - comprises approx 1100 spacious rooms in 10 quality buildings for employees

So, at Dubai South, you can find different living options as per your budget and requirements.

Affordable Prices

As you know that residential properties in Dubai are preferred by real estate investors & home buyers from different parts of the world, thanks to affordable price luxury. But if you are looking for affordable then your search ends at Dubai South. Here you can buy or rent any apartment, townhouse or villa at a very affordable price than Dubai Downtown. 

Excellent Location

There is a misconception in the mind of many people that Dubai South is positioned away from the Downtown but they are wrong. Dubai South enjoy connectivity from Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road & Emirates Road and proximity to Dubai Investment Park. As well as, commuting to other areas of Dubai takes only a few minutes.

Transportation And Parking

There are two major things here, first- you don’t need your vehicle here, thanks to the adequate transportation system like the F55 bus service, Red line Metro and taxi services that can take you where you want to commute within Dubai. 

Second thing is - if you have your car or cars then parking here won’t be a problem. Every villa, townhouse & apartment here their dedicated parking. 

Educational Institutes In The Area

Families with school-going children prefer to move into a location where schools & institutions are nearby, and from this point of view,, Dubai South is a perfect region. 

  • Best Schools in Dubai South
  • Dove Green Private School (British curriculum)
  • Greenfield Community School (American curriculum)
  • British Columbia Canadian School (Canadian curriculum)
  • Bright Riders School (Indian curriculum)

Shortly, you can expect more best schools & quality institutions in this area. 

Facilities And Amenities On Offer

Any neighbourhood considers incomplete without basic & civic amenities. So you can expect every facility & amenities that are necessary to lead a luxury & comfortable life ranging from community parks to retail stores & shopping malls.
