Emaar Beachfront Waterfront Community

Emaar Beachfront is a magnificent island community by Emaar Developer. This 10 million sq. ft development is one of the top off-plan projects in Dubai at present. Lifestyle, in this community, is entirely dedicated from the luxury standpoint and that is why residents here will enjoy living extravagantly at every step. 

This superluxury community is intended with apartments, penthouses as well as podium villas and townhouses. Emaar Beachfront Community boasts nearly 10,000 residential units in 27 towers and you can get your preferred choice between 1-4 BR Apartments & Townhouses and 3-4 BR Villas. 

As the community is developed close to Dubai Harbour you will enjoy the beautiful, serene, calming view of the beachfront straight through your abode. Imagine waking up every morning with the calmness of the beach and enjoying and sea view holding a cup of hot coffee in your hand. And in the evening, the cool breeze from the Arabian Gulf is more relaxing and a perfect treat for the end of the day before hitting the bed. It would be amazing to catch a good sleep while hearing the sound of the calm waves coming from the beach, living life amidst such beautiful surroundings is indeed very soothing.

Master Plan Of Emaar Beachfront

If I talk about the master plan of this huge community then you will be surprised by knowing that, this modern development consists of residential as well as a hotel complex. Situated next to Dubai Harbour, not only it is adorned with beauty but every single facility like retail and transportation systems is well developed to provide an ease of life to the residents also. 

Moreover, residents here will enjoy an Infinity Pool, Gyms, Multiple Recreational Spaces, and 24/7 Concierge Service in its residential complex. Emaar Beachfront is smartly developed close to the Arabian Gulf where you can enjoy the panoramic view of the beaches as well as get free access to it. 

Properties Available In Emaar Beachfront

For a perfect holiday destination Emaar Beachfront is the best option and mostly the property here is preferred as a holiday home by the emirates. If you are looking for a second home in Dubai then you can explore this place. There are manifolds properties choices like apartments, penthouses, villas, and townhouses you will get in this community. So, choose wisely the resident types where you can unwind for a lifetime.

Places to Hangout in Emaar Beachfront

If something fun strike your mind then you can explore these places in this community. 

  • Golden Mile Galleria -70 outlets with verities of options like supermarket, fitness club, health club, etc
  • Nakheel Mall – sprawled over 4.5 million sq. ft, 350 retail stores give you a whole new leisure experience along with filling your appetite with delectable food in the dining place
  • Al Shorfa Restaurant & Café, Mom’s Food Restaurant, and Olive restaurants are some exotic waterfront dining space 
  • Budhha Bar, Barsati Toro Toro, and ICON Bar & Lounge are the best options for nightlife 
  • Spend some time in the sand, sun, and beaches at Marina Beach, Pine Beach, and JBR Beach

Major Attractions Nearby

As the community is part of Dubai Harbour not only you will enjoy the view of a natural Harbourbut there are other major attractions nearby also. Discovering those places is just breath-taking for instance:

  • How about exploring the first man-made island built in the shape of a palm tree- Palm Jumeirah!
  • Get all your branded items at one shopping destination – Dubai Marina Mall
  • Dubai International Marine Club

Sales vs Rent Trends In Emaar Beachfront

Due to waterfront properties, the community is highly desirable amongst end-users as well as investors. And there are plenty of housing options such as apartments, penthouses, villas, and townhouses available in different sizes you can go with your choices. 

The demand for an apartment for sale is increasing and you can get approx. AED1.1M to AED 3.8M on a 1 bed beachfront apartment whose sizes range between 839 – 1,144 Sq.ft. 

  • For 2 beds it will cost you  AED 2.2M -AED5.6M. 
  • 3 bedroom apartment for sale will cost AED 3.4M -AED 8M
  • 4-bed apartments will cost AED 7.5M -15M

Similarly, you will get a good rental price for a 1-bed apartment that starts from AED 110K. For a 2-bed apartment, it will cost you around AED 160K to AED 345K Yearly.

Major Connectivity You Must Know

Ain Dubai is a popular landmark close to the Emaar Beachfront community. Otherwise, you can easily reach Dubai Marina within just 9 minute drive. Downtown Dubai is 22 minutes via Sheikh Zayed Road. Palm Jumeirah – 15 minutes drive away. Dubai International Airport is just 26 minutes to reach.

FAQs

Q- Where Is Emaar Beachfront Located?

Ans- It’s a part of Dubai Harbour located at the intersection of Palm Jumeirah and Bluewater island, Dubai.

Q- What Types of Properties Are Available In The Emaar Beachfront community?

Ans- You can easily find villas, apartments, townhouses, and penthouses in this community.

Q- Is There  A Mosque In Emaar Beachfront?

Ans- Yes. Mohammed Bin Ahmed Al Mulla Mosque, Musabeh Al Fattan  Mosque, and JBR Mosque are within just 8-10 minutes away.

Q- Which Other Places Are Infor Worship At Emaar Beachfront Where I Can Go?

Ans- There are various places that are close distance for worship. You can go to a church. Seventh-Day Adventist Church, Coza Church, Dubai is 11-16 minutes driving distance.  Also, Shiva Temple, Shiridi Sai Baba Mandir, and Shrinathji Temple are just 30 minutes distance. 

Q- Can I Get a School For My Kids In Emaar Beachfront?

Ans- Yes, you can and there are various options with different curricula. Here are some schools nearby the community:

  • Icademy Middle East 
  • Gyanberry Dubai 
  • The International School Of Choueifat

Further, if you are looking for nurseries near the Emaar Beachfront community then here are the top choices:

  • Monroe's Nursery
  • Fun & Play Nursery 
  • Family and Friends Nursery

Q- How Far Is Dubai Downtown From Emaar Beachfront?

Ans- It is about 22 minutes’ drive away via Sheikh Zayed Road .

Q- How Far Is Dubai International Airport?

Ans- Dubai Airport is nearly 26 minutes from Emaar Beachfront .
