A Complete Community

Though Dubai is divided into more than 20 communities and each of them targets different needs, but if we talk about Falcon City of Wonders then undoubtedly it is a complete community where the signature park, commercial facilities, sports venues, recreational centres, educational institutes, impressive home designs with 30,000 residential properties and seven wonders of the world makes it a signature of Dubai where the resident will always stay content.

Community Divided Into Themes

This area is divided into four themes named as- Santa Fe, New World, Aegean and Andulusia which again has a purpose to style different types of properties and pick a diversified culture where you get a perfect feel of living.

  • Andalusia features Spanish architecture from the golden Andalusian era with detached villas
  • Aegean creates Greek atmosphere and presents 4 bedroom townhouses, additionally, 3, 4 and 5 bed villas are there as well
  • Santa Fe comes with Mexican style detached villas
  • New World offers North American style townhouses

So can you imagine while living in Dubai you can have European as well as American exposure? And that is where it showcases 50 nationalities.

The picture is yet not over- there are more international communities in Falcon City- Western Residence South, Western Residence North, Eastern Residences villas North/South.

  • 214 residential units in Western Residence North adjoining the Pyramid’s Park
  • Western South comes with 215 villas and 131 townhouses
  • Eastern Residences villas North/South adds off plan 5 to 6 bedroom villas
