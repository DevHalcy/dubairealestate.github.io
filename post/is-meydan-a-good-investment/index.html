Is Meydan A Good Investment?

Meydan is the best community and a visionary concept of Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum. Presenting best options for investment in residential, commercial & hospitality developments.

About Meydan

Meydan is not just a community or district in Dubai but a dream project of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum. This district was launched in 2007 and within this short time span became a new city centre of Dubai. Spread over 45 million square feet (Approx 918 Acres) of land in the heart of MBR City, nearby Ras Al Khor Road and directly accessible from Dubai Downtown.

Since 2002, the district has seen huge infrastructural developments in terms of different options for live, work, play, dine and shop with the establishment of many high-end projects, some of which have been delivered, some are under construction, and many are in the pipeline. This community is not home to many world-class residences, hotels, racecourses, golf courses etc.

If you are thinking - Is Meydan Dubai A Good Investment or Not? Then here we have listed some of the best reasons to invest here.

Meydan is Freehold Area

Strategic Location

This community is just few minutes away from Dubai International Airport, Sea Ports and important locations of Dubai. If we particularly talk about International Airports then there are approx 2.5 billion people are just 4 hours and 5 billion are just 8 hours away from here. So many visitors & businessmen from GCC countries like India, Pakistan, Middle East, Africa & Europe comes to Dubai and needs accommodation nearby Airport & business destinations. And communities like Meydan is the perfect choice for them.

If we talk about connectivity of Meydan community from the rest of Dubai then this community is also perfectly located nearby from major landmarks of Dubai.

  • Downtown Dubai - 10 Minutes
  • Dubai Creek & Al Khail Road - 10 Minutes
  • Dubai International Airport (DXB) - 15 Minutes
  • Palm Jumeirah - 25 Minutes

A Vibrant Community

This is a best community for the people who are setting up their business in Dubai. A Free Zone in Meydan provide you with access to an exclusive community that impel growth through innovation and collaboration. A central location of Dubai provide a perfect ecosystem to start and grow any business.

For the residents this community is holding everything that is require to lead a luxury & convenient life. Meydan district comprises freehold residential developments, best schoos, multi-specialy hospitals, parks & open spaces and amenities. It would not be an overstatement to say that - Meydan is city within city.

Lifestyle And Offerings

Every best community in the world primarily focuses on the amenities and experiences that potential buyers get. For example a family with childrens tend to focus on spacious homes, security, parks & open spaces nearby. Meydan City is set to become a favorite hotspot for families and tourist in Dubai with best services, high-end facilities and amenities to cater to diverse cultural backgrounds.

Shopping And Retail

For every lifestyle & daily necesities of the inhabitants there is world-famous Meydan One Mall inside the community.This mall is one of the most anticipated developments that redefine best retail experience not only in Dubai but in the world. Major highlights of Meydan One Mall are:-

  • Approx 550 retail outlets
  • 13, 200 square meter hypermarket
  • Approx 200 F&B outlets
  • 21-Screen cinema
  • A long indoor ski slope & 12,000 quare meter wide Winter Village
  • Dancing Water Fountain
  • 28,000 sqm entertainment experience
  • 5,430 sqm Medical Center
  • Luxury Plaza Hotel
  • Lifestyle Canyon & 20,000 sqm office tower
  • 112500+ car parking spaces
  • Entertainment And Leisure

For the entertainment & leisure needs of the inhabitants of Meydan there are lots of options to enjoy. Meydan have one of the largest artificial lagoon in the world, this 7 kilometer long waterbody have clear water and lined by palm trees, running through commercial developments, hotels, restaurants, parks and residential developments.

Meydan Golf Course is there for the Golf lovers, this 9-hole golf course is spread over 7, 412 years with natura lakes. Working on pay & play concept there is no need of membership, a best concept for visitors.

How can we forget to mention world-famous Meydan Racecourse and Grandstand boasts longest horse racing tracks spanning over 1.5 kilometres with a spectacular facility that can accommodate approx 80,000 visitors.

Schools And Universities

To address one of the biggest concern of the families “proximity from best education institutions”, Meydan comprises some of the best schools within its premises in different sub-communities.

North London Collegiate School is positioned inside Sobha Hartland. Spread over 38,000 sq. meter area and offering its students international curriculum standards dedicated to promoting the holistic development. A best options for the residents of developpments in Sobha Hartland.

Another best school here is Hartland International School presents UK curriculum to the students. This school also positioned within the Sobha Hartland community and just few minutes away from other residential developments with Meydan.
