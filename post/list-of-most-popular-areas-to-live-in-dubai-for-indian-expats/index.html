List Of Most Popular Areas To Live In Dubai For Indian Expats

Dubai is a hub of comfortable living where luxuries, comfort, food, stay, travel and more come complimentary. So for the Indian expats here are the most popular areas of Dubai

Dubai is an international destination where people from far over the globe come. The exquisite living, premium lifestyle, high-end brands, lavish cars, spectacular clothing, dazzling jewelry, better standards of living, attractive jobs, lip-smacking food, and whatnot is here in DUBAI to let a person live life king-size.

Dubai is the land where people from the whole world come and live. Due to manifold benefits people especially from India come and settle here in Dubai it is one of the most favored ones. A popular business hub that stands out from the crowd and provides opportunities for ex-pats. There is no place in the whole world like Dubai i.e., a popular tourist attraction and a business center.

It takes just a couple of hours from India to reach this mesmerizing land, but this place has some of the places that are just perfect for ex-pats traveling from India providing an ultra-modern living. Let us count on some of those areas of the popular global city.

Al- Furjan: Get Vibrant And Friendly Living

One of the most promising locations in Dubai i.e. located along the Sheikh Zayed Road is an outstanding option for ex-pats. You can get the best living here with the upcoming promising options. Here you can get the best blend of residential and commercial properties providing the highest standards of living. Here you can obtain the best 1 Bed, 2 Bed, and 3 Bed apartments and more properties. It is one of the fastest-growing developments with shops, salons, supermarkets, and more. Here one can enjoy the long drives and opulent living.

Bur Dubai: Dramatic And Elegant Dubai Is Here

Being part of the original city of Dubai, Bur Dubai is located on the western side of Dubai Creek. It is counted as one of the largest residential areas where ex-pats can live a peaceful life. Wonderful living is waiting for you at this place. It is home to long-term Indian residents because the aura of this place is phenomenal. The countless dining options to let your taste buds enjoy the lip-smacking food, historic buildings giving you options to roam around, temples and mosques for families, and much more.

Sheikh Zayed Road: Busiest Road Of Dubai

One of the main highways of Dubai and the road that connects you to the rest of Dubai. It has many high-rise buildings and apartments providing comfortable living options. It is a place bustling with robust infrastructure making it proficient for working professionals. It stretches to many parts and is the home of residential skyscrapers.  Sheikh Zayed Road holds many properties and the location that has shopping and dining options with exquisite nightlife holding some cafes, restaurants, gaming sections, and more.

Dubai Investment Park (DIP): Green Living Community

A perfect and the most environment-friendly development that is designed under eco-friendly policies focusing on providing a healthy and clean environment. It gives the perfect blend of residential, commercial, industrial, and recreational developments. Expats get ready to lead a high-quality and smartly planned live in this quirky location. It is radiated over an area of 2300 hectares offering world-class infrastructure and facilities. Each corner is dotted with an abundance of luxuries.

MBR City: Planned Mixed Use Development

Mohammed Bin Rashid City (MBR City) is one of the most outstanding mixed-use developments. Here you can get the amalgam of apartments, villas, and other properties with modern designs. Connectivity from here is smooth and you can easily reach many prominent locations in a jiffy. It holds some eye-catching attractions with manifold leisure activities and is surely the biggest shopping center. Then it has the world’s largest man-made lagoon and soon going to have the biggest Meydan One Mall.

Mina Rashid: Man-Made Cruise Terminal

Expats generally come to Dubai for a comfortable stay and lifestyle, well Mina Rashid is unbeatable in this. A location that provides a wide-range of high-end retail options, world-class dining, multiple entertainments, and much more. It is counted as one of the largest ports in Dubai and has a prestigious address. The rich coastal destination is just a blink away from many prominent locations like Downtown Dubai and Burj Khalifa. What more is needed for a comfy living? It is the place for the future.

Dubai Marina: Affluent Living Place

A place that is home to varieties of living options. Here you can stay in lavish hotels, gorgeous apartment buildings, and more. The skyscrapers of the place catch all the attention and the magnificent view around. The whole area is surrounded by the Gulf and Atlantis Island giving an eye-soothing experience. The artificially designed spot and the place that leaves you awe-struck has not just the panoramic views but the class residential area.  So, one of the most amazing options to be booked now.

Arabian Ranches: Upscale Community Where Comfort Awaits

A beautiful place that garners all the attention and the location Sheikh Zayed Road i.e. prominent in every sense. It is a gated villa community that has lavish living options. Here you will find apartments, villas, and other residential options imbibed with a gush of greenery. The whole area is covered with a wide green cover and more attractions like the clubhouse, community center, worship places, and more. There is so much here to enjoy and comfy living. Discover the coolest living segments in the shimmery golden sand.

Palm Jumeirah: Giant Place With Multiple Comforts

Oh, what is not to be said and mention about Palm Jumeirah? Dubai has prominence with the name Palm Jumeirah. The tree-shaped island is known for its glitzy hotels and apartments. The global market gives restaurants with world wide cuisines. It is one of the most daring developments where you can make a splash at Atlantis, swim with sharks and dolphins, have boozy brunch, splash tours, and a chic beach club to chill and relax.

Wrapping up the blog

I can say that there are countless Indians who relocate to Dubai in search of better career options and lifestyle but still they want to feel at home wherever they stay; this is the reason why Dubai can be counted in the top place and moreover the above-mentioned places giving the homely places. So if you are moving to Dubai or still in plans then choose the right area from the mentioned list and build yourself in a positive location
