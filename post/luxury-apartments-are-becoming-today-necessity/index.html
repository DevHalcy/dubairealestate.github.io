Luxury Apartments Are Becoming Today’s Necessity

Luxury Apartments in Dubai are becoming the hot topic of discussion. Everyone is demanding the apartment living because of the multiple benefits. In this blog, I have mentioned what can be obtained after owning luxury apartment.

Luxury word is taking the center stage in the market. There are loads of things that come under this one word- LUXURY, especially in the real estate market. Luxury Apartments have taken the world by storm and have garnered huge attraction in the last few years. People are spending huge amount to gather the good things attached with luxury apartment living.

The need for luxury in today's world is greatest than ever. It has become a living symbol attributing to a few factors like modern expansion, population growth, and the need for better living. Residential properties are becoming better than ever and bringing benefits that nobody wants to leave.

Initially luxury word can be a bit intimidating and many people think they can’t afford it. In contrast, luxury apartments have so many perks that let you disregard the apartment's cost while saving money. I think luxury brings you comfort and peace and this one word has so much inside it apart from just spending a huge amount and if you can afford then what’s the harm in it?

Here I have put some of the reasons why luxury apartments are today's life.

Avail the benefits of countless amenities imbibed in one home

Luxury apartments offer amenities that are far superior to what can be obtained in a standard apartment. Amenities in the home provide the true essence of living conveying luxury and providing comforts all the time promoting fulfilled and healthy life. The luxury apartments come with a variety of amenities such as a gym, yoga room, swimming pool, high-quality fittings and fixtures, fitness studio and fitness classes, rooftop or garden patios, grilling and BBQ stations, pool and hot tubs, pet-friendly spaces, entertainment areas, etc.

Panoramic views soothing the eyes and soul; live the best life

Luxury apartments are mostly found in luxurious locations only that come with robust infrastructure and good views. These apartments come with spacious balconies, wide windows, terraces, etc. providing an enjoyable view and experience the urban and green living. Another important aspect of an apartment is the view from its windows. In terms of specifications and amenities, a project may be genuinely luxurious. However, the view gets more attractive with the surroundings and with the things not commonly seen.

Connectivity and Civic Amenities boosting the location benefits

In luxury locations, you have access to manifold conveniences that a person always looks forward to in life. Luxury apartments take you close to the amazing neighborhood. Such a neighborhood is important for a better living experience. Luxury locations provide you with a better tenant experience, leading to active living, lip-smacking restaurants, entertainment venues, leading industries for better job opportunities, etc.

Stay with the like-minded people

Communal living has become one of the most common aspects of today’s living. People all over the world want to avail the best living and somehow community living has become an aspect of that. Community living provides great things to do and enjoy and lets you meet different people. You will find like-minded people here and the amenities that develop the community high-end. One can enjoy a positive environment and meet & greet new friends. Communal living is a win-win situation where amenities and good people combo can be obtained.

Feel safe in the atmosphere covered by round-the-clock security

Luxury homes come with better and improved security systems and you feel safer there. Round-the-clock security options are added in luxury homes or communities. You are completely safe in and out of the premises with CCTV Surveillance, Security Guards at the door, Alarm systems, and more. There is no worry to the residents living inside as the security makes sure of the resident's safety. Then you have more security options like community access, security card, key access, doorman, etc. These additional security features let you relax and you lead a better living when you know you are protected.

Enjoy the job within the housing community

We are living in a world that is run by technology. Each sector is blooming with the help of technology. Every luxury housing community comes with a clubhouse that provides you with so many good options to enjoy. The luxury apartments come with beautiful business centers offering job facilities which mean many residents can work in the home environment only. Then the communities also arrange some events to maintain excitement in living. Well, luxury apartments in Dubai have so much to offer you.

Neighbors not peeping into your life anymore

What can be better than having an access to the amazing neighborhood? You are getting the best of both the worlds like modern living and an attractive neighborhood! WOW; what else do you need? A good neighborhood along with high-caliber tenants lets you enjoy the same environment. Then you can access local parks, civic amenities, coveted attractions, restaurants nearby, entertainment venues, etc. A luxury apartment adds so many good things to your living.

Get the awe-inspiring lifestyle

Home imbibed with countless comfortable features

Luxury apartments come with an abundance of features that just not add lavishness but comfort in living. You are completely in your zone without any disturbance

Earlier people were hesitant in selecting apartments because of the hindrance to their privacy. But now the builders are recognizing the needs of homebuyers and how much they are craving privacy. Now builders make sure that no two balconies face each other, every balcony should have panoramic views, special utility areas are designed in the apartments adding convenience to the residents, and separate rooms for domestic help, widely spaced apartments each occupying one floor, individual terraces boosting even greater privacy and more.

Data Comparison Of 2021 VS 2022

The luxury housing market is very dynamic and always presents something out of the box. Buyers are completely favoring the luxury housing segments and suddenly in the last two years, luxury housing is witnessing a peaking interest. Dubai market has witnessed a prolific growth from 2021 to 2022 in the supply of luxury homes. In 2021 apartment units transacted were 15,863 and this increased to 25,705 in 2022 taking a leap of 62% and the total value of property type counted in 2021 was 21,725,558,029 and this increased to 47,663,633,256 in 2022 giving an increase by 119%. This is the data just from the first half of the year; imagine how beautiful growth the whole year will witness.

FAQ (PROVIDING EXTRA KNOWLEDGE)

1. How Much Does A Luxury Apartment Cost In Dubai?

Luxury apartments in Dubai cost between AED 1,000,000- AED 90,000,000 and the rents are also high depending on the count of facilities being provided. Apartments can be higher priced than this depending on the location and amenities.

2. What Is The Cost Of Living In Dubai?

The cost of living in Dubai in 2022-23 is increasing from AED 3,435.71 to AED 3,477.89 for a single person and AED 11,863.31 to AED 11,958.62 for a family of four. This is the average cost of living that is easily affordable.

3. Which Are The Luxury Apartments In Dubai?

Emaar Beachfront Beachgate by Address, EMAAR Hills Park Dubai, AZIZI Riviera at Meydan One, etc. is some of the top-luxury projects in Dubai presenting a beautiful blend of apartments varying in sizes. These apartments are located in outstanding communities offering comfortable amenities.

4. Which Are The Top Areas To Buy Luxury Apartments In Dubai?

Downtown Dubai, Jumeirah Bay Island, Dubai Creek Harbour, Palm Jumeirah, etc. are the area to find high-end apartments. These areas are performing phenomenally well witnessing the demand of sale and purchase.
