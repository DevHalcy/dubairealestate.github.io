1) International City Dubai (Freehold Area)

Dubai International City creates an ideal hub for businesses, tourist and alluring theme based architecture which is captured over 8 million square meters and the main look & feel of the area showcase the traditional Middle East. And mainly, it is planned by Nakheel Properties where the major presence is of apartments followed by villas, townhouses, duplexes and land. And the area is considered as the most affordable attraction for buyers where within the budget they can opt for any type of property inside.

In addition to this, more projects are going to be added here where the community will accommodate over 60,000 residents, and for the ease of living the area comes with commercial and retail space to bring the basic needs closer to you.

  • SCHOOL- American schools (25 min) & English National school (15 min)
  • HOSPITAL- NMC Clinic (6 min)
  • RESTAURANTS- Wokyo Noodle Bar (20 min) and Shabu Shabu-Nine Squares Restaurant (3 min)
  • NIGHTCLUB- Goldenque (20 min), Societe Nightclub (33 min) and The Hideout Dubai (33 min)

Q- How much does it cost to buy property in Dubai International City?

It depends what you buy in Dubai International City, but if you go for a studio apartment then it starts from AED 270,000 over 514 Sq. Ft. to maximum AED 520,000 so from an affordability point of view, International City never fails to serve properties for the new home seekers, so you will easily find a wide range of properties best for small family size.

Q- Where exactly is International City Dubai located?

It is situated in the Al Warsan region of Dubai, opposite to the Dubai Central fruit & vegetable market. 

Location advantages of Dubai International City

  • Dubai International Airport- 16 min
  • Burj Khalifa- 25 min
  • Dubai Frame- 24 min
  • Dubai Mall- 23 min
  • Jumeirah Mosque- 29 min

Q- Which metro is near to International City Dubai?

No metro is available at present, but with the help of public transport to can come down to Rashidiya Metro station.

And it is established in 1997 where it is scattered over 2300 hectare area and it creates a perfect work life balance with the active 4200+ companies that attract employment and real estate market.

Nearby Facilities In Dubai Investment Park

For 2 bed apartment with 4 baths over 1,561 Sq. Ft. area it will cost you around AED 700,000 in Dubai Investment Park and the range can go up depending on the size of the apartment you buy. Whereas, villa in International City will cost AED 1,200,000 (size 2,050 Sq.ft.) so the home buyers can opt for the best option, and the mentioned price would vary on the location, size of the apartment, facing, builder and more.

It is placed next to Jebel Ali Industrial Area and next to Dubai Production and Jumeirah Golf Estates which is an ideal location well connected to the Burj Khalifa, Dubai International Airport, Sheikh Zayed Road and more.

Location advantages of Dubai Investment Park

Discovery Gardens is situated within the district of Jebel Ali so it is referred to as Jebel Ali Discovery Gardens. The name says it all ‘Garden living ambience’ where it comes with zen, Mediterranean, Mesoamerican, mogul and cactus that creates a proper aroma of green theme to maintain appropriate landscaping with different activities to bring family and kids together.

To make it the best residential community the site comes with 26,000 affordable units where studio, 1 and 2 bed apartment are considered as the top choices, for better livability the community adds 3 medical centres and 200 retail shops to have access to the day to day services.

In addition to this, the community is divided into 5 themes:-

  • Mediterranean
  • Mogul
  • Zen Cluster
  • Contemporary
  • Discovery Gardens Pavilion

Where Mediterranean is the most popular area offering family-friendly ambient, and other than this, more zone that differ in its look & feel.
