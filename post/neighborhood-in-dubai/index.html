Best Neighborhood in Dubai For Living: According to Age Groups

There are many best communities & regions in Dubai for living, but every community has its features & aspects that make it a perfect fit for a particular age group. So here in this blog, we have mentioned some best regions in Dubai with suitable age groups.

Dubai is considered one of the few cities in the world and the only city in the middle-east that brings together metropolitan-style luxury & creativity, a city where everyone even foreign visitors feels safe. All these facts increase the property demand from all over the world, whether for work, study, real estate investment or long-term stability. Countless reasons for everyone to settle in Dubai but not everyone can achieve this goal. But still, every year people from different parts of the world come to Dubai and settle here. 

In this article, we present to you a group of the most sought-after residential areas in Dubai according to age groups ranging from 18 years to 45 years and divided into 4 categories (18 - 24 Years, 25 - 34 Years, 35 - 44 Years and 44 Years and above).  So you can choose the best area according to your age or future plans. 

We understand your feeling that searching for a residential neighbourhood according to your age seems crazy, but it is a very normal aspect in Dubai. And there are many areas or communities in Dubai that are suitable for particular age groups, due to the amenities and services that are dedicated to particular age groups. So, let's explore this in detail…

The most popular areas for People Aged 18 and 24 years

This is the youngest age group and people who come under this group are generally bachelors and only a few are married. Most of the people at this age are at the initial stage of their careers and earning not too much, so they prefer small apartments for rent. The given table shows the most popular areas for apartments for ages between 18 and 24 years who are often looking for small space properties available for rent option.

According to this table, Jumeirah Village Circle tops the list as this is more affordable, then Jumeirah Lakes Tower and Dubai Marina last because this community is more expensive.

Jumeirah Village Circle

This is one of the most popular affordable & popular areas, especially in the middle of age-group between 18 - 24 years old. And there are two biggest reasons first is affordability and second is the availability of recreational facilities & essential services like parking spaces, swimming pools and gyms etc. As well as Dubai university is near and numerous schools & institutions are just a few minutes away. 

There are good options for apartments for rent in Jumeirah Village Circle. The average rent of a studio apartment is AED 30,000 yearly; if you prefer 1 Bed apartment for rent then the average rental is AED 45,000 per year.

Jumeirah Lakes Towers

Also known as JLT, hosts a wide range of services & amenities that attracts home seekers, especially youth. Many serviced apartments in Jumeirah Lakes Towers offer a high standard of living. Here you can find the best options in apartments for rent. The average rental of a studio apartment is AED 40,000 yearly, and for 1-bedroom apartments, the average rental is AED 58,000 per year.

Dubai Marina

This is the best place to live in Dubai if you want to experience the best lifestyle. This community offers the best lifestyle, with energetic nightlife, an abundance of dining options and a couple of the tallest towers in Dubai. All these things and more make living at Dubai Marina a golden opportunity for all age groups, especially from 18 to 24 years old. 

Home seekers can find the best apartment options at the best annual rental. The average rent for studios in Dubai Marina is AED 57,000 and the rental for a 1-bed apartment is AED 91, 000.

The most popular areas for apartments are for ages between 25 and 34 years

People who belong to this age group are about to get married or are already married most of them are well-settled in their life. So this table shows the best region for apartments, especially for this age group.

1 & 2 Bed apartments perfectly fit in their budget and requirements. The table indicates the best regions for ages 25 - 34 years.

AED 57, 000 AED 36, 000 Dubai Silicon Oasis

Dubai Silicon Oasis

Even as this region is famous as a free zone for the companies affiliated with IT sectors, which makes Dubai Silicon Oasis a perfect choice for young working professionals. So most of the residents here are between the ages of 25 - 34 and working somewhere in Dubai Silicon Oasis. 

Dubai Silicon Oasis is known as the free zone for companies affiliated with the technology sector. Well-developed transportation system and ease of access to the rest of Dubai make it more desirable.

Most people here preferred 1 or 2 Bed rental Apartments. The average rental of 1 Bed Apartment in this location is AED 36,000 and for 2 Bed Apartment, the average rental is AED 57,000 annually.

Al Barsha

This neighbourhood is the second most preferred region in Dubai among small families & working professionals as there they get many advantages like reasonable price rental and a wide range of facilities & services and the best housing options. As this region is suitable for the 25 to 34 years group and provides them with everything they expect ranging from connectivity to luxury lifestyle features. 

Here you can rent a 1 Bed apartment with an average rental of AED 46,000 and for 2 Bed Apartment, you have to pay AED 64,000 annually.

Downtown Dubai

This is not just a community or region but the heart of Dubai. Youngsters from the age group 25 - 34 years prefer to live in this location because most individuals of this age group prefer to live in the heart of the city. Downtown Dubai is mostly characterized by presenting a contemporary lifestyle and a world-famous tourist destination, that’s why this is considered Dubai's most expensive real estate destination. If you are looking for a rental apartment in Downtown Dubai then 1 Bed apartment here will cost you AED 95,000 and a 2 Bed apartment will cost you AED 175,00 annually. 

The most popular areas for villas are for ages between 35 - 44 years

Generally, people of this age group belong to small families (husband, wife and kids) or large families (husband, wife, kids and parents) and the majority are employees in good companies or self-employed and prefered bigger units like villas. The given below table shows regions that are perfect for ages 35 - 44 years with villas consisting of 3 to 4 rooms.

4 Bed Apartments 3 Bed Villas Areas
-- AED 155, 000 Springs (Alyanabia)
AED 112, 000 AED 89, 000 Mirdif
AED 277, 000 AED 170, 000 Arabian Ranches

 

This table represents the top areas for ages between 35 to 44 years, here Springs ranked first, Mirdif ranked second and then Arabian Ranches. Individuals of this age group prefer to live in villas whether for rent or buying. 

Springs (Alyanabia)

This is one of the best residential communities that offer everything small or medium families are looking for. Here you can find luxury villas at very reasonable price rent with modern amenities & features, the best structural design, and community facilities. Not to mention the wide range of recreational facilities and luxury and other amenities.

If we talk about 3 Bed villas for rent then you have to pay AED 155,000 annually. There are a few 4-bed villas and other options are also available for rent at a very attractive price.

Mirdif

Mirdif is one the most desirable community in Dubai, particularly in this age group, thanks to reasonability in price and inclusion of basic services and luxury facilities that boost the lifestyle of the residents. As well as, a peaceful atmosphere, green surroundings, and lots of options available for housing, whether apartments or villas for rent or sale in Mirdif at very attractive prices. A region in Dubai where the inhabitants enjoy social stability and security & safety, as well as comfort & peacefulness, and a comfortable distance from the main Dubai. This region is easily accessible to Dubai International Airport and many commercial districts are developments are nearby. 

There is an abundance of options when it comes to renting a luxury villa. A 3 Bed Villa here is available at  AED 89,000 per year. On the other hand, if you want a 4 Bed villa then you have to pay only AED 112, 000 annually. 

Arabian Ranches

This gated community was launched in 3 phases by Emaar Properties. The most desirable development in Dubai is in the middle of international buyers & investors for the best standard of living and high-end community amenities. There are many residential developments within this community that are off-plan and many are ready to move. So, here you can find villas for sale and rent as well in different configurations. A 3-Bed Villa in Arabian ranches is available for rent at AED 170,000 and a 4-bed villa in Arabian ranches is available at AED 244,000 annually.

The most popular regions for villas for ages over 45 years

At the end part of this blog, we are talking about mature people who have achieved success in their life and these people generally belong to a group of 45+. This age group usually comprises large families, most of these people are businessmen or employees of big communities looking for luxury villas for rent. They generally prefer to live in the region shown in the table below.

4 Bed Apartments 3 Bed Villas Areas
-- AED 142, 000 Damac Hills
AED 180, 000 AED 139, 000 Cities (Modon)

 

The upper-mentioned table shows the top residential regions & communities for people 45+. Damac Hills is the first choice, then by Mudon, and Emirates Hills is the last.

Damac Hills

Damac Hills is one of the best communities in Dubai from reputed real estate developer Damac Properties. Many home seekers & real estate investors looking for property in residential developments of Damac Hills. This community is located in Dubai next to Al Qudra Street and provides a luxury lifestyle for the inhabitants. The facilities & amenities within this community vary between recreational and waters sports one of its kind in Dubai. The community is divided into three parts, Akoya Park, Akoya Drive, and Trump International Golf Club.

For 3 Bed Villas for rent in Damac Hills, you have to pay an average rent of AED 142,000 annually. This is the best price if you compare the amenities & features of this community with nearby developments.

Cities (Modon)

This region has gained huge attention from home seekers in Dubai who are looking for rented villas. Located on the peripheries of Dubailand nearby the intersection of Al Qudra and Emirates Streets. This is a popular property in Dubai thanks to its wide range of ready-to-move-in villas & townhouses and other residential options. A perfect choice for families thanks to its advantageous location and ease of access from Dubai International Airport, presents calmly and vitality at the same time.

A 3 Bed villa in Mudon for rent will cost you AED 139,000 annually, on the other hand, a 4 Bed Villa is AED 180,000 annually. 

Emirates Hills

Last but most in-demand residential neighbourhood in Dubai, especially in the middle of 45 years and above. Developed by reputed Emaar Properties and part of Emirates Living and shares its peripheries with many areas like plains, springs, and lakes. Emirates Hills is also one of the best freehold development in Dubai with the best & luxury living options, peaceful surroundings, well-developed neighbourhoods & ease of connectivity. 

If you are looking for rented villas in Emirates Hills Dubai then you have to pay AED 348,000 annually. If you are looking for off-plan property in Emirates Hills then there are no options in this category. Here you can find the best options from top real estate developers in the best freehold communities. As well, reasonable prices and aftersales services & assistance are free. 

Conclusion

These are the best communities in Dubai for different age groups. But it doesn’t mean that only a particular age group can reside in a particular region or community. There is no restriction and anybody can live anywhere as per their budget & requirement.
