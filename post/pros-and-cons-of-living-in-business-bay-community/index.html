Pros Of Living In Business Bay

1. A PERFECT OPTION FOR FAMILIES

As there are larger units in Business Bay so one can see families living, and as it is a professional place so one can make a perfect balance between the work and personal life. And for true comfort- schools, dedicated parks for kids, health care centres and lots more options give you better space to live in.

2. BETTER LOCATION

For any location, its connectivity matters a lot and as Business Bay is a central location so connectivity comes automatically where Downtown Dubai, Dubai Opera, The Dubai Mall and lots more options are well approachable.

Some Major Sports With Driving Minutes

  • Palm Jumeirah- 22 Min
  • Dubai South- 40 Min
  • Miracle Garden- 20 Min
  • Downtown- 10 Min

All in all, it keeps you connected way better and then rapid transportation is also an edge.

3. ENJOY THE VIEW

As Business Bay is situated on the banks of the Dubai Canal so it offers a breathtaking view of the skyline where the experience is completely worth living, and when you are with your family you enjoy every bit of it, after all, you will be the part of Business Bay the leading communities presenting you the best of the best view.

4. SURROUNDING BY A YOUNG CROWD

With the fast paced vibes you will get to see the pool of young professionals working that brings better way better exposure wherever you look around, also living with such group is always an edge to experience. And with this, you always get business and job opportunities often.

So welcome in a diverse population and several events hosted by Bay Avenue, so you will interact better with the other residents too.

5. ENJOY UPSCALE LIVING

Meet a high end lifestyle at Business Bay where top notch hotels, restaurants, and lots more give you all new experiences and exposure and everything within the vicinity not just saves your time but also gives you heaps of options of explore. A perfect amalgamation of nightlife and social gatherings.

Besides this, Downtown is the most considerable filler where you can explore all major attractions with a distance.

6. ACCESS TO TOP LEVEL HOTELS 

With the grand ambient and lavish structures you can also access to the premium range of hotels- JW Marriott Marquis Hotel, (Second tallest hotel in the world), Taj Dubai, Steigenberger Hotel and more options topped around Business Bay where if you have business meetings you can take the most of these hotels and also for leisure you can plan your stay anytime.

7. BETTER PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION

Apart from the fancy dining and major attractions, public transportation is always the main concern for the reason that a personal car is not always a good option, good transportation system matters a lot- so metro, taxis and a great network of buses make it easy peasy for the residents to move around to any place.

Even for work better connectivity is required, so when you are part of Business Bay daily commuting will never trouble you and always be into your budget.

8. AMENITIES & SOCIAL INFRASTRUCTURES 

Amenities and social needs are considered as the main part of lifestyle because without this living is completely bland, so when you are here you get the freedom to access parks, jogging tracks, hotels, restaurants, playgrounds, events and more.

Other than this, for the local needs, you have lots of options that coveryour daily needs, so here it goes what all you will get when staying Business Bay.

  • SCHOOLS- Maple Bear Pre School, Blossoms Nursery and Canadian Kids Nursery
  • CLINICS- Emirates hospital and Medcare Hospital
  • TEMPLES- Jumeirah Mosque, Al Ghafoor Mosque and Burj Khalifa Mosque
  • SUPERMARKET- Al Maya supermarket, Zoom mini marts and West Zone Supermarket
  • MALLS- Bay Avenue Mall and The Dubai Mall
  • RESTAURANTS- Tong Thai, Oberoi and Cuisnero Uno

These options and lots more give you good exposure to everything within the distance where with families these are the basic needs to ameliorate the lifestyle.

So  the above points showcases the benefits of living in Business Bay and now let us move to its Cons where exactly, they are not the drawbacks, but something you should be aware if you plan to live here.

Cons Of Living In Business Bay

TRAFFIC COULD BE AN ISSUE 

As it is a leading and functional district, so traffic is something obvious and being a resident here you should know this before and it is always better to take out that extra time to reach any destination.

SKYSRAPER CITY

If in case your choice is not to live in high rise buildings, but here you will just find this and not something where you can find peace, rather it is more happening and crowded.

The traffic and ambience could trouble you, but if are ready to live in a city like atmosphere then nothing could be better than this.

CONCLUSION

So with lots more pros of living in Business Bay than cons it welcomes the home buyers to experience an active lifestyle and to see the real side of Dubai, even for investors it is a great choice because its high demand will serve you good rental and eventually high ROI (maximum it went to 6%).
