PROS OF LIVING IN DAMAC HILLS

Check out 9 pros of living in DAMAC Hills where it gives you real points to consider for your investment, and read them all:-

1. TRUMP INTERNATIONAL GOLF CLUB

Are you a golf lover? Then this fits so good and well because the community comes with Trump International Golf Club, so here you will get all new experiences where professionals add extra value to the Golf Club.

This Golf Course is planned over 73,000 yards of area designed by Gil Hanse to add Trump branded 18 holes with a par 71 championship golf course and also exclusive dining options promise an ultra luxury experience to each golfer.

2. POOL OF OUTDOOR FACILITIES 

The community comes with sufficient recreational facilities where tennis, badminton, tennis courts, football field, skate park, gardens & lakes, amphitheatre, gym and lots more options are there in the community that gives you good enough choices to experience.

The main idea is to bring everything together and let them experience the active side because the sedentary lifestyle is already having a detrimental effect on each one of us, so it becomes essential to be a part of such a lifestyle.

3. AWAY FROM THE CITY

Actually this could be a drawback for some but actually, it is a benefit because it keeps you away from hustle and bustle of the city where you find a peaceful environment and then less crowd ensures better access to the outdoor facilities, don’t you want to be in a peaceful environment? Then this gives you better surroundings.

4. GREENERY AROUND

As mentioned above the community adds 4 million square feet of lush greenery where its green field gives you a pleasing environment and its pollutant free ambience makes it better than others.

With this, parks and lakes add extra beauty to the site where you can stroll around and imbibe the freshness of air, chirping for birds and better sun view so this collection brings you closer to nature and lets you cherish the true side of it.

5. AFFORDABLE COMMUNITY 

This is what makes it attractive enough for both investors and home buyers where the starting price of studio apartment is AED 484,000 with the average rental price AED 33,000, so this is surely a lucrative offer where both the users can make the best out of it.

What type of property do you get here?

  • APARTMENTS- Studio, 1, 2 and 3 bed starting from AED 484,000 to maximum AED 1,756,000
  • VILLA- 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 bed villas with a starting price of AED 1,671,000 to a maximum AED 4,854,000

So comparing this price comes out as a golden opportunity for those who are having a limited budget, but still want to experience the true side of Dubai, then DAMAC Hills would the right choice.

6. IMPRESSIVE ROI

There is a mindset that if you go for an affordable property, then you might not get the right ROI, but that is not the case, here you will get an unexpected ROI and here it goes the list:- 

  • STUDIO- 6.1%
  • 1 BED- 6.1%
  • 2 BED- 5.5%
  • 2 BED VILLA- 6.1%

So these are the maximum ROIs that you get when becoming a part of this community, and also would like to say, there are top areas in which you may not find such ROI, so isn’t it profitable for you? Then better not to miss the opportunity of investment in DAMAC Hills.

7. POPULAR COMMUNITIES INSIDE 

This community comes with sub communities to maintain different looks & feel and then to have different types of properties, so out of all Topanga, Pelham and The Field are the top communities that will give elite living exposure and a bigger home to live in.

Other than this there are more communities which are surely not a bad choice, but it might give you a basic living experience, so everything depends on your choice and need, rest the whole community DAMAC Hills have its own experience.

8. LUXURY HOTELS 

With the better residential ambience, the community is laced with luxury hotels as well where Golf Veduta, Carson, Golf Terrace and Artesia are some of the best examples that give you a luxury experience in case you have a mood to stay in a hotel with your family.

9. COMES WITH YOUR DAILY NEEDS

Along with comfortable living, needs are important too so here the basic facilities are approachable that ensure right close to:-

  • SUPERMARKET- Geant Express Supermarket, Spinneys Supermarket and Carrefour 
  • WORSHIP- Shirdi Sai Baba, Emirates Baptist and United Christian Church
  • SCHOOL- Jebel Ali School, CreaKids Nursery and Blossoms Mudon
  • HOSPITAL- Mediclinic Parkview Hospital, Aster Clinic and Core Care Polyclinic
  • RESTAURANT- TukTuk Thai Cuisine restaurant, Maria Bonita Taco shop and Abatjour Bistro

So each of them gives you a better advantage in terms of living because they all are part & parcel where without them it is trouble.

CONS OF LIVING IN DAMAC HILLS

After knowing the advantage of this community, now it is time to know about the cons that give you a better picture of the community, so check them below: -

1. NO PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION 

With every comfort in DAMAC Hills, its commuting comes out as a challenge because there is no bus stop or metro station, so here you need to have your car to commute all over Dubai. 

And with your own vehicle travelling will not be challenging for you.

SOME LOCATIONS WITH DRIVING MINUTES 

  • Dubai International Airport- 32 min
  • Burj Khalifa- 25 min
  • Abu Dhabi- 30 min

CONCLUSION

After grabbing the pros and cons of living in DAMAC Hills, it comes out that along with the travelling issue which is easily manageable by personal transportation there are heaps of benefits that make this an ideal choice for both the investor with minimum investment and end users with a family friendly environment.
