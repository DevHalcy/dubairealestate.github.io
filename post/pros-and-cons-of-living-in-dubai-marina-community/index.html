Pros Of Living In Dubai Marina

Below you will find out 6 pros living in Dubai Marina that offer you better reasons to understand the community and even to think on- is Dubai Marina a good investment?

1. Be A Part Of The Attraction

It is truly a blessing to live in a place that acts a major attraction, so Dubai Marina is enough in itself where nearby beach, JBR offers flyboarding, paddle boarding, jet skiing and lots more experience for everyone.

Other than this, Dubai Marina Walk, Bluewaters island, Palm Jumeirah are some strong places where one can hang out anytime as it takes no minute to reach. So if you are someone who involves in different activities, then this is going to be a cherry pick of every resident. 

2. Best Ever Range Of Apartment

This community comes with a high range of apartments where one will experience the top level living space based on the futuristic floor plan offering the maximum scope of using the space.

So with 200 residential towers in Dubai Marina, it gives you the tallest buildings serving an uninterrupted and panoramic view of the city and further the buyers can enjoy its maximum ROI, currently, it has witnessed 6.4% ROI for studio apartments and 6.2% for 1 bed apartment.

3. Active Lifestyle 

Every project comes with a high range of facilities where the gym, swimming pool, kid’s centre, sports courts, jogging track and more are within the project that creates an active & energetic environment, and after a sedentary day, it is important to engage in different activities where you can make the best of your time with your family. 

4. Easy Public Transport 

Its prime location gives you way better access to the major parts of Dubai where its closeness to Sheikh Zayed Road, city’s arterial highway and then Dubai Metro stations (DMCC and Sobha Realty) creates better networking for you to stay connected to the major zones.

And rest if any place is not accessible then taxi or your personal transport can be a good way to commute. 

Easy To Reach Destinations 

  • SUPERMARKET- Al Maya, West Zone Supermarket and Waitrose Supermarket
  • WORSHIP- Masjid Al Rahim, Shiva Temple and Guru Nanak Darbar
  • SCHOOL- Dubai British School, The Springs, Raffles Nursery and Horizon International 
  • MALL- Dubai Marina Mall and local stores- Miniso, H&M and Charles & Keith
  • RESTAURANTS- Pier 7, Shades, Atelier M, Cargo Dubai and more

Besides this, it gives you a comfortable drive you various spots such as Jumeirah Lakes Towers (2 min), Downtown Dubai (20 min), Emirates Golf Club (2 min), Al Maktoum International Airport (30 min) and Dubai International Airport ( 25 min).

5. Known For True Entertainment 

It is said that there is no shortage of restaurants, bars, nightclubs, malls and more branded options wrapped in this community that will keep offering you the luxurious side of Dubai and living in the main spot of Dubai is always an edge, don’t you think?

Then why to miss the opportunity of living in Dubai Marina?

6. Events Throughout The Year

Surely these are some obvious perks you experience in such a top notch community of Dubai, so different events where it allures families to come together and have the blast- outdoor craft markets, handicraft exhibition, experience Iftar meals, firework, children’s events and more.

The motto is to bring everyone together and create a new side where for every member has something to explore, and actually for entrepreneurs, it is a good place to meet the elite class and even display their products at the event.

Cons Of Living In Dubai Marina Community

Now it is time to check out the cons to get a fair idea on both sides because when you plan to live in any community awareness is the key to a comfortable life.

Traffic 

As Dubai Marina is a tourist spot so traffic would create some mess for you and for this you need to take out extra time to reach your office or any local visit.

Trouble Of Noise

As it is a tourist spot where many supercar riders would create noise, but it is not too much that it becomes out of control. 

Lack Of Parking 

Relax! For residents it is not a problem rather visitors might find the scarcity of the parking space, so it is more from tourist’s concern.
