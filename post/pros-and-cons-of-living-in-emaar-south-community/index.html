Pros And Cons Of Living In Emaar South Community

Looking for a property in Emaar South, but cannot decide whether it is a good decision or not. Don’t worry read about these pros and cons of living in Emaar South and take the best decision.

Emaar South at Dubai South is the future of Dubai. As its name suggests this community is positioned in the Southern part of Dubai adequately away from the city’s humdrum,  a peaceful location with modern infrastructure. If we talk about  Emaar South then it is a golf theme community with features an 18-hole golf course and presents golf-facing Villas, Townhouses & Apartments at a very attractive price. This community is under construction and different developments will be delivered in different years. 

At this time total of 8 residential developments are available for sale in Emaar South. 

  • Expo Golf Villas Phase III - 3 & 4 Bed Villas 
  • Expo Golf Villas Phase IV - 3 & 4 Bed Villas 
  • Expo Golf Villas Phase V - 3 & 4 Bed Villas 
  • Urbana - 2 & 3 Bed tiered and standard townhouses
  • Urbana III - 2 & 3 Bed ready-to-move-in townhomes
  • Golf Links  - 4 & 5 Bed Villas 
  • Golf Views - 1, 2, 3 & 4 Bed Apartments
  • Expo Villa VI - 3 & 4 Bed Villas

The most popular residential development mentioned above is in Emaar South, which has a wide selection of villas, townhouses and apartments to choose from.

Highlights of Emaar South Community 

  • 15,360 Apartment Units
  • 53,000 sqm Retail and Dining Space
  • 18-Hole Championship Golf Course
  • 25 Neighbourhood Parks
  • 22,700 Residential Units

We know that these upper mentioned points are not sufficient to decide that - buy a unit here is the best decision or not. So here we have provided unbiased pros & cons of living in Emaar South. Read these points and decide.

Pros of Living in Emaar South

Low Price High ROI

This community is positioned in the middle of peaceful surroundings & golf course makes it more attractive to home seekers & tenants. Villas & Apartments in the Emaar South community are continuously in demand among visitors, golf lovers and locals who enjoy leading a peaceful, luxurious life surrounded by green surroundings.

Investing in Emaar South is start at a very affordable price if you compare other properties from Emaar in other locations of Dubai. And the best thing is that in this small investment price you will get a high ROI just like other Emaar Developers. 

Prime Location

One of the biggest pros of living in Emaar South is its prime location near Dubai Exhibition Centre (Dubai Expo Site). Residents of this community also benefit from the proximity of the golf course, as well as the community recreation centre and a beautiful park. Nearby retail & dining options has increased the joy of living in this community. 

From here UAE’s biggest Al Maktoum International Airport is just 5 minutes away. As you know Dubai’s hassle-free residency visa & free-hold properties scheme and geographical position Dubai is a maximum of 8 hours of flying time away from 1/3 population of the world. So, this airport aims to handle approx 2.6 million people annually by 2030. 

Emaar Expo Golf Villas are positioned next to the main street of the community with parks and a community centre and Dubai City Centre is not far away from here.

  • 5 Minutes From Al Maktoum Airport
  • 15 Minutes From Expo 2020
  • 25 Minutes From Dubai Marina
  • 35 Minutes From Downtown Dubai
  • 45 Minutes To Dubai International Airport

Ease of Access To Schools & Institutions

This point is very important for the parents and yes living in Emaar South comes with access to the best schools & institutions in Dubai. 

If you prefer British Curriculum for your kids then Dove Green Private School is located just 25 minutes away at Dubai Investment Park. This location is also home to Greenfield Community School which presents an American curriculum and if you want a Canadian curriculum then British Columbia Canadian School is the best nearby choice. For the Indians, there is The Bright Riders School. 

And if you are looking for higher-level education for your kids then Dubai Knowledge Park is there with many world-class universities. 

Part of Dubai South (A well-planned district by the Dubai Government)

Emaar South is positioned within the peripheries of Dubai South. This district is a big master-planned project developed by the Dubai Government. 

There are separate zones for commercial, aviation & retail activities, and thousands of shops, luxury homes, and office spaces are part of Dubai South. As the district has developed under the support of the Dubai Government & Ruler itself, so there is no point to doubt on the prospect of this development. As well as, the administration of Dubai is famous for its impeccability and strict rule & regulations. So no matter what is promised by this district or Emaar south is guaranteed to be delivered. So again, the best ROI & rental are guaranteed.

Best Interior Design

Villas are available for sale with tailor-made interiors for the residents. Every unit is finished in for muted neutral tones to perfectly fit different types of furniture, as well as each room, is spacious & fill with natural light. A perfectly designed patio & balcony will make sure you get a green & unhindered view of the surroundings. 

Your Dream Lifestyle

As a resident of Emaar South, you have lots of things to do and enjoy the green park is here to walk, jog or just sit peacefully. At the Rove hotel, you can enjoy dinner or lunch there with your family. A community centre is there with lots of amenities like a gym for workouts, a swimming pool for adults and a play area for kids. You can also enjoy world-class sports facilities, Golf Course and floodlit driving etc. 

Cons of living in Emaar South

Dealing With Real Estate and Landlords Can Be Tricky

Real estate deals like buying or renting are very tricky not only in Emaar South but the entire of Dubai, and this is especially for foreigners who are not aware of the local rules & regulations. So, it doesn't matter who much experienced you are in real estate, you must take the help of a real estate agent or real estate company, who can find a better deal and assist you with paperwork. If you need a recommendation then Dubai Housing is the best real estate company in Dubai, help you find the best off-plan freehold property from reputed real estate developers. And the best thing is that you don’t have to pay any fees for their services. 

Lots of Construction Activities Going On

As you know that Dubai South and Emaar South both are still under construction. So at this time, you have to compromise with some basic features of living. In many places, the construction of roads and the development of many communities are still in progress. Public transport is not sufficient, roads & highways are nearby but the metro is very far easy. All in all, the ongoing construction of homes and transportation are the biggest cons of Emaar South.

Conclusion

Buying a home in Emaar South is the best option for real estate investors & home buyers. This community have everything that a normal property buyer expect like green & peaceful surrounding, well-developed infrastructure, amenities & facilities and connectivity from the rest of the city. There are some cons of living here too but these are only a few. So, you can decide what is better for you.
