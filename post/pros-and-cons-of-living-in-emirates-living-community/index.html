What is Emirates Living?

Emirates living is a modern lifestyle community that brings a series of outdoor leisure and it brings a serene nature filled sanctuary and its premium villas with 8,659 options and lots more scattered over 52.2 million square feet of lush greenery.

Pros Of Living In Emirates Living

Below you will get through 9 pros of living in Emirates Living that expresses every benefit to understand the worth of the community and to find out what all is accessible?

1. Meet Freehold Gated Villa Community

Experience the large scale villas based on the futuristic layout to get core access of the space and live in a big home. And as the community is loaded with more than 8,000 luxury villas, so you have the freedom to choose the best out of it.

2. Freehold Zone

This sought after community comes with freehold properties which means you will get 100 % ownership of your property and also curtail the extra expenses like ground rent and more.

So for options, you have :-

  • 2 & 3 bed semi detached villas
  • Low rise apartments

3. Enjoy Community

This community comes with 5 different residential community that gives you all new experience as they are based on different themes, so here come:-

  • Meadows Dubai
  • The Springs
  • Emirates Hills
  • The Lakes
  • Hattan

So these 5 communities stand differently in terms of theme, options and feel, so which one would you like to pick?

For those who are looking for affordable accommodation “The Springs Dubai” is the best option serving sports facilities, poo, green space and more for a comfortable and easy stay.

4. Exceptional Amenities For You

As the community is divided into 5 different themes so it comes with heaps of facilities where barbecue area, community parks, lakes, playground, swimming pool, sports facilities, a green space area, The spring ambience and lots more amenities and recreational activities inside the community gives you and your family a grand explosure of everything.

5. Better View For You

View of your property matters a lot so this community gives you a lake view to those who enjoy water bodies around with the gush of cool air and then park view to get wide view of green belt area offering you a pollutant free environment and wherever you look it gives you peace and rush free environment.

6. Forever Be  A Part Of Masterpiece

This 50 million square feet area showcases the multi billion master project by EMAAR properties where the builder maintains its class, position and quality, so this way you always be a part of an exotic development where it makes your lifestyle better and satisfying.

7. Not Too Old

This community is completed in the year 2006 which makes it not too old a community and the on going development with the label of EMAAR properties add real worth to the community.

You have trust and always be a part of an updated community, and most importantly, it comes with 5 different themes which again makes it best in itself because community with sub communities bring different culture and theme of living

8. Good Transportation System

Don’t you have car?  No worries because here in. Emirates Living you will find robust public transport both metro and bus stations are nearby that further connecting you to the major parts of Dubai.

How much will it take to reach the important spots of Dubai?

  • Business Bay- 20 min
  • Downtown Dubai- 19 min
  • Palm Jumeirah- 13 min
  • Dubai International Airport- 31 min
  • Sheikh Zayed Road- 9 min
  • MBR City- 21 min

So ideally it will take 30 min maximum to reach any destination in Dubai and most importantly, its location is another important factor that comes out as a comfort.

9. Every Need On Your Demand

For the basic needs and requirements the site comes with daily access here you have everything within the distance which works as a bigger benefit of living in Emirates Living community.

What All Do You Get?

  • SUPERMARKET-Carrefour, Aswaaq Mart, Choithrams and The Springs Souk
  • SCHOOL-Dubai British School, Dubai International Academy, Raffles Nursery and more
  • CLINIC-Noor Al Shefa Clinic, The Springs Souk Pharmacy and Mediclinic Meadows
  • MALL-The Meadows Souk and The Springs Souk
  • RESTAURANT-Nineteen Restaurant, Links &Monty’s and Gastropub
  • BEACH-JBR Beach, Marina Beach and Barasti Beach
  • LEISURE OPTIONS-Jebel Ali Racecourse, Dubai Marina Mall and Montgomerie Golf Club

So the above mentioned options give you smooth access to everything which is your needs, fun and necessities, and when you have everything in the vicinity it makes your lifestyle way easier and better.

Cons Of Living In Emirates Living

Now let us move to its cons or disadvantages which is just for your awareness.

Not Too Fancy

So the community might not give you that too fancy experience, but more on a sober side where you can have a peaceful ambience rather than experiencing the hustle & bustle of the city, this could be a drawback for some but can be an advantage for those who are seeking for calm & composed environment for their families.
