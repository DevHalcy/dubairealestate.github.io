Pros Of Living In Falcon City

In this segment, you will find the 10 pros of living in Falcon City that show you a better picture of the community and also will give you the right answer on- Is Falcon City of Wonders a good investment?

Get Families Around

As the community welcomes 30,000 residential projects with different sizes & designs so here you will be surrounded by families just like you and never feel isolated, so with too many families around you get healthy and easy atmosphere all day.

Enjoy Theme Living

Falcon City of Wonders comes with 4 different themes and that includes Santa Fe, New World, Aegean and Andalusia where every theme holds a different meaning, so you can live in a theme fits in every way.

  • Santa Fe-  Mexican style detached villas
  • New World- North American style
  • Aegean- Greek style 4 bedroom townhouses
  • Andalusia- Spanish detached villas

So it brings different cultures together and gives you the freedom to live in a theme space as per your choice.

Family Friendly Community

Falcon City of Wonders is designed for families only where it offers grand living space and then the range of amenities and attractions make it an ideal choice where families never get bored of anything.

So are you ready to experience the most comfortable housing? Then nothing beats this come here to explore all new side.

Live In Bigger Home

As the community comes with the combination of villas & townhouses, so here you will have bigger space to live in and then the attractive floor plan gives you better usability of the whole space, so always feel proud and live like a king in Falcon City of Wonders.

Maximum ROI

One of the biggest and important reasons to be a part of Falcon property, the ROI goes up to 8% which is 100 per cent profitable for any buyer, and this again makes it an ideal destination for the buyers to be a part of a place that secures your capital

Freehold Zone

Properties here are freehold which gives you 100% ownership and makes you the complete owner of your property and this way you don’t have to pay ground rent or any other expense, so a great way to save your unnecessary expenses.

Enjoy Transportation

Bus service is the most convenient option for the public where they have bus stations- 103 & F30 and rest your own car will add good support to your travelling.

And the community is located in Dubai Land, Wadi Al Safa 5 and approachable from Dubai Highways so within the reach you can meet any distance such as Dubai International Airport (23 min), Sharjah (40 min), Business Bay (25 min), Burj Al Arab (23 min) Dubai Mall (20 min)

Local Needs Near Falcon City Of Wonders

For local needs, you will find everything over here to set up a comfortable environment where you have easy accessibility of everything.

Here it goes what all you can access:-

  • SUPERMARKET-Thomsum Supermarket, Lifco Supermarket, Bluemart and more
  • CLINICS-Ace Medical Centres, Aster Clinic, magnum Gulf Medical Centre
  • Mall-Souq Extra, Dubai Outlet Mall and Cityland Mall
  • RESTAURANT-Russo’s New York Pizzeria, Oregano Restaurant and Doner Deli
  • SALON-Gents Salon, The white Lily beauty Salon and Aroushi Beauty salon
  • SCHOOL-The Aquila school, GEMS Heritage school and Dunecrest American School
  • WORSHIP-Shirdi Sai Baba Mandir and Automech Gurdwara

With these, there are lots more options available near this community where you will access everything that helps you in your daily life.

Heaps Of Activities

As mentioned above there are heaps of facilities and recreational activities, so you and your family will have fun exploring all of them- Swing yoga, Oryx fit Dubai, Universal fitness, Community area, Falcon City Bootcamp and more options create better exposure for you, so that means you don’t have to drive too long to give your kids a better platform to explore and have fun as everything is better over here.

Exceptional Attractions

The community gives you an impressive range of attractions which makes this exclusive in every way, so Dubai Hanging Gardens of Babylon, Dubai Great Wall, Taj Arabia, Dubai Great Wall and lots more options give you whole new experience.

And living in such community is a blessing itself, so this is the major advantage of Falcon City of Wonders in Dubai.

So the above mentioned pros of living in Falcon City give you robust reasons to be a part of the community and have a peaceful and happy life.

Cons Of Living In Falcon City

It is not really a disadvantage, but something that you should know it before.

Modern Lifestyle

If you expect an ultra-modern lifestyle, then Falcon City wouldn’t be a great option because here you will rather have sober living with all modern facilities, but will not see the real charming side of Dubai.

Conclusion

Keeping everything together it comes out as the best place for families as the bigger home and heaps of facilities promise a comfortable and cozy lifestyle and with this, one disadvantage that it lacks a modern lifestyle still makes it the best community for home buyers.
