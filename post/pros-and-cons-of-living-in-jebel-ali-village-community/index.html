Pros And Cons Of Living In Jebel Ali Village Community

Jebel Ali Village is an old district in Dubai, redeveloped in 2013 as a master community. Just like other communities, Jebel Ali has also some pros & cons, let's explore them in detail.

About Jebel Ali Village

Jebel Ali Village was established in 1977 as an accommodation for construction workers. These construction workers were brought there to develop this location as an industrial town with an airport & port. Redevelopment work of this community as a modern project had begun by 2008. And now this community is known for its elegant design of villas & townhouses. 

Jebel Ali Village as a master community comprises 4 separate communities. And every community have their own amenities & features like parks & open spaces, ponds, vegetable farm, swimming pool and sports facilities. Positioned nearby Ibn Battuta Mall and nearby Sheikh Zayed Road offer ease of access to the major locations of Dubai.  Apartments & Villas are available for sale here with world-class experience and proximity to everything that is required to live a comfortable life. 

This is about Jebel Ali Village and most home buyers & investors already know about it. But there are many hidden pros and cons of Jebel Ali Village that you must know about. So here in this blog, we are presenting these points. In the first part of this blog, we only talk about the Pros, in the second part we talk about the cons and then we end with a final thought. Let’s start…

Pros of Living in Jebel Ali Village

You Will Be Well Connected To The Main City 

As you know that in the main regions of Dubai parking space is very costly and hard to find. But in Jebel Ali Village every residential development comes with dedicated parking space for residents & visitors.

This location is just a stone's throw away from Dubai Exhibition Centre. As well as, the nearby Jebel Ali metro station (The M1 Metro) is connected to 29 stations from Rashidiya Metro Station to Jabel Ali Metro Station. There are many bus stops around the corner of  Jebel Ali Village location that connect passengers to places all over Dubai.

If we talk about travel time from important locations of Dubai then:-

  • Dubai International Airport - 40 Minutes
  • Downtown Dubai - 35 Minutes
  • Dubai Parks & Resort - 10 Minutes

Best Options For Buying & Renting

This community is divided into 4 sub-communities and offers 290 units as 2 Beds, 3 Beds and 4 Beds villas & townhouses but some developments have 5 Beds & 6 Beds options for big families.

If you are looking for rental accommodation here, then it can be hard to find. As people who already live in this location tend to stay long term thanks to the peace, amenities and open space you get for your money. Even Jebel Ali Village is a small district in Dubai but within the small area has a worship place, amenities, retail stores & other civic amenities. According to the local real estate agent, on average, renting a 1 Bed apartment in Jebel Ali Village will cost you approx AED 65,000 a year, whereas rent for 4 Bed Townhouse or Villa is AED 225, 000

On average, renting a three-bedroom standard villa will cost no more than Dh114,000 ($31,035) a year, whereas rent for four-bed villas is Dh126,000 per year.

Worship Places For Everyone 

Even though Jebel Ali is a district where Muslims are in big numbers but people from different religions & ethnicity are also living in perfect harmony. So, there are numerous mosques spread over different parts of the community, famous mosques in this region are:

  • Masjid Ruqaya
  • Masjid Handalath bin Abi Umar
  • ebel Ali Beach Masjid
  • New East Accommodation mosque

For the residents of other faiths, there is a certified Sikh Gurudwara, a Hindu temple nearby, and several catholic churches are accessible from this location. 

There Are Many Shopping Options Within & Nearby Jebel Ali Village

Everyone like shopping and in families there are lots of things that we frequently required for example groceries. You can go to Carrefour for your monthly grocery need. There are many other stores & supermarkets inside the community like :

  • Meena Centre FZE
  • Nesto Hypermarket 
  • Village Hypermarket 
  • Sultan Hypermarket 
  • New Parco Supermarket
  • Malabar Supermarket
  • Salam Al Madeena Supermarket
  • Zabeel Oasis Supermarket

How can we forget to mention Spinneys, Mcdonalds' Drive-Thru, and Ibn Battuta Mall?

The list is very long, so you don’t have to go far to buy anything if you live in Jebel Ali Village. 

Best Schools & Nurseries Are Nearby

For families with school-going children, there are lots of best options in schools & institutions. There are many nurseries & primary schools within the community:

  • Little Woods
  • Bubbles
  • Giggles Nursery
  • Jabel Ali Village Nursery
  • Hamilton Primary Schools

There are many best & recognized schools in the community with different curriculums like Delhi Private School (Indian Curriculum), The Winchester (UK Curriculum), and Jebel Ali School (UK Curriculum). So this is one of the best pros in this community. 

Lots of Community Events 

Jebel Ali Village hosts many weekly, monthly & seasonal events to foster the sentiments of community living. Anybody can participate in these events in The JA Recreational Club. There are many family events are also organized in this community to meet & socialize with other residents of the community 

These are the pros of living in Jebel Ali Villas. We know there are many other advantages of living in this community, but we cannot describe all here. Now come to the cons of living in Jebel Ali Village. 

Cons of Living in Jebel Ali Village

Traffic Congestions During Peak Hours

Jebel Ali Village is well-connected to other parts of Dubai through roads & highways but if we talk about public transportation then it is almost negligible. Yes, bus stops are there but outside the community, the metro station is also a couple of minutes away from here. Many of the residents here are working in different parts of Dubai and are dependent on their personal vehicles, and there can be some amount of congestion during peak hours.

And this is the biggest & only cons of living in Jebel Ali Village. 

It is very important to consider the pros & cons while looking for property in Dubai, but don’t forget to consider future market prospects. Jebel Ali Village is positioned in perfect proximity to the Downtown, with the expansion of Dubai peripheries the price of properties in Jebel Ali is expected to rise. This is the best time to property investment in Jebel Ali Village. 

Conclusion

Jebel Ali Village is one of the best communities in Dubai with ease of access to Sheikh Zayed Road and Al Maktoum International Airport. There are numerous pros and only a single con of living in this location and property price is expected to rise in the future. So this is the best investment opportunity too.
