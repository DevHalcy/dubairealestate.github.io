Pros Of Living In Palm Jumeirah

Now let us get into its key features to give you a fair idea about the advantages of living in Palm Jumeirah

1. Palm Jumeirah captures waterfront views

The area is wrapped with the water itself, so it becomes obvious to get waterfront view, but you need to understand how interesting it is to be a part of Palm Jumeirah forming the world’s iconic pattern palm at the coast of the Arabian Sea.

And it is scattered over 560 hectares area forming a true water body with sparking effects and proper illumination that captures the attention of every passerby. Most importantly the area is away from the hustle & bustle of the city so it upholds a peaceful environment with an uninterrupted view of the artificial island as well as imbibing its freshness all day.

2. The Palm Jumeirah Island Is A Self-Sufficient Neighbourhood

This makes it worthy and interesting too because for any community its self sufficient neighbourhood with the accessibility of everything makes it worth living and after all, there is no point in living in an isolated community.

So when you are you get a better approach to every single thing from a doctor, to retail shop, to school to food outlets and lots more within the distance to stay engaged and without moving much you have grand choices in a row.

3. Here you find every type of configuration 

When you get everything in a single place when why to move anywhere else? So here in Palm Jumeirah you will find the amalgamation of villas, townhouses, apartments and hotels with better ROI rates to allure more investors and create better investment zone. 

DO YOU WANT TO KNOW HOW MUCH WILL COST YOU?

  • Apartment average sale price is AED 778,000 and the maximum AED 33,158,000
  • Villa average sale price is AED 2,396,000 and the maximum AED 45,572,000

And for the maximum ROI, 1 bed apartment and 1 & 2 bed villas come out as lucrative options with 5.5% and 5.3% respectively. 

4. Promises you a luxury living in Palm Jumeirah

The area is widely known for its outstanding designing and concepts where every project is equipped with the high range amenities consisting of gym, swimming pool, cafes and lots many options works as a true leisure.

With plethora of luxury hotels and exposure of Palm Beach it uplifts the overall feel & experience of the user. In fact, the premium range of villas & resorts on the crescent creates real richness of luxury.

5. Active options for you

Mouth watering dining options- Club Vista Mare, 101 Dining lounge and Nobu and for exclusive leisure water activities you can enjoy the series of sports and rides.

Beyond this, The Sea Lion Point, The Aquaventure waterpark, Dolphin Bay and Lost Chamber Aquarium are some great entertainment points best when you go with your family because this experience is for all ages. 

Cons- You Should Know Every Side Of It

Cons are not the real drawbacks, but some realities that you should be aware of if you plan to become a part of it, so here we go!

1. Still Not Connected To Dubai Metro 

Monorail is there that connects to Palm Jumeirah and it is mainly for the tourist, but for the local connection you might not find any route by Dubai metro, so there are bus and taxi services for in & out commuting.

2. Ongoing Construction  

There are many off plan projects where the construction work has started and because of this you might have to face noise and dust, so be prepared for this become it becomes a turn off for you, other than this, roadways are confusing where it might be difficult for you to navigate, but yes, initially it is a problem and then you know the routes.

3. Luxury Lifestyle To Pay More 

For this you are already cleared, its premium lifestyle and expensive properties will ask for more penny and yes, the price is comparatively higher than other areas of Dubai.

And somewhere you know this, so it might not be a big drawback, but something you should know before it gets difficult for you to manage the expense.

CONCLUSION

So the whole idea of presenting these pros & cons is to make you clear about the worth of living in Palm Jumeirah so comparing both pros & cons it makes Palm Jumeirah a good investment for the investors because of its tourist touch.
