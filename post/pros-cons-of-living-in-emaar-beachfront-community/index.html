Pros & Cons Of Living In Emaar Beachfront Community

Emaar Beachfront is an exclusive community with many attractive features & best housing options. But there are some pros & cons of living in Emaar Beachfront, that you should know before taking any decision.

About Emaar Beachfront

Emaar Beachfront is the best community located nearby Palm Jumeirah. This community was launched in 2018 by Emaar with a residential tower Beach Vista. The Beachfront will constitute 27 towers, at this time community has 9 towers each with access to the private beach, and many are in the pipeline.

Highlights of Emaar Beachfront community

  • Offers eye-catching views of the Arabian Gulf and Palm Jumeirah on one side and the city skyline on the other
  • 1.5 Kilometer long beachfront only for the residents
  • Just walking distance away from Dubai Marina
  • Comprising total 10,000 units 
  • Retail mall spread over 13, 000 sq. m.
  • A Miami-inspired community featuring high-rise towers with access to the beach
  • Direct access to Sheikh Zayed Road provides the best entry and exit options.

But, everything in this world is not perfect and there are some pros & cons that come with living in Emaar Beachfront. Here in this blog, we have described some best pros & cons.

Pros of Living Emaar Beachfront

Every residential development from Emaar always comes with many advantages but there are some special pros of living in Emaar Beachfront that we are going to mention here. 

You Enjoy Beachfront Living

As its name defined “Emaar Beachfront”, here you will enjoy your living in a home that is beach facing. You can walk or play on the sandy shore whenever your want. So, if you are looking for a luxury home with a beach then you should consider this community. 

You Get a High ROI Yield

As you know that entire Dubai real estate offers the best return on investment. But developments in Emaar Beachfront have attracted more investors & home buyers thanks to the better return. Given below are the best residential developments in this community with better returns. 

Grand Bleu Tower - 1, 2, 3 and 4 Bed Apartments that are completely sold out. This is the world’s first residential building designed by Elie Saab. At the time of launch starting price of this development was AED 2.1 Million with a potential ROI of 6%. 

Address Residences The Bay - Comprises branded apartments, penthouses and townhouses with starting price of AED 2.4 Million with an expected ROI of 6%. The project is completely sold out and the handover date is 2026. 

Beachgate By Address - Comprising branded 1, 2, 3, and 4-bedroom apartments, 4-bedroom penthouses and duplex penthouses. Starting price of this development is AED 2.9 Million, completely sold out and the handover date is 2026. 

Beach Mansion -  These are completely sold out 1 to 4-bedroom luxury apartments, townhouses and penthouses with starting price of AED 1.6 million with a potential ROI of 6%. The handover date is 2025. 

Advantages of Perfect Location

This is one of the biggest pros of the Emaar Beachfront community. Positioned in the middle of Palm Jumeirah and Dubai Marina, so the residents of this community enjoy proximity to several yacht clubs, malls, restaurants and recreational zones. As well as, nearby Sheikh Zayed Road makes you commutable from the rest of Dubai.

  • Dubai Marina - 1 Minute 
  • Downtown Dubai - 15 Minutes 
  • Dubai International Airport - 20 Minutes 
  • Dubai Exhibition Centre - 25 Minutes 
  • Al Maktoum Airport - 45 Minutes 

You Enjoy Stunning Views

It is clear by the name “Beachfront” every home in this community presents eye-catching views of the beach, sea and green. So with a home in Emaar Beachfront, you not only get high-end amenities, luxury specifications and all but stunning views are also guaranteed, and this is also an advantage of living here. 

You Get the Best Property Management

Every Emaar residential development comes with the best management services, in order to provide you best value for the money. So if you are a foreign real estate investor and looking for off-plan property in Dubai then you must ensure that the builder is offer property management services. Basically, property management service refers to the company that takes full responsibility for building maintenance after handover.
With your home in Emaar Beachfront, you will get the best property management services from Emaar itself. 

Guaranteed Timely Possession 

One of the biggest concerns of home buyers is a delay in possession. But you can check the history of Emaar Developments in Dubai or different parts of the world, this developer is known for timely possession. And if you buy a property in Emaar Beachfront off-plan development then here you can expect the same. 

You Get High-Quality Materials

Emaar Beachfront community is recognized for the usage of the best quality material and only branded fittings & fixtures in every unit. If you are quality conscious and looking for a weather-resistant home, then you should not go anywhere. 

You get lots of things to do

Whether you just prefer to sit & enjoy the views or want different physical activities, this community have lots of options to enjoy. Positioned at the centre of the Harbour, you can take a boat and go fishing, just take an easy stroll on the sand of the beach with the benefit of private beach access, play in the swimming pool, workout in the fully-equipped gym, play with your children in the play area or ground and many other.

Cons of Living in Emaar Beachfront

While living in Emaar Beachfront has many pros, there are a few cons too that every home buyer & investor should consider. Cons are given below

High-Maintenance Cost

This is the biggest advantage of living in Emaar Beachfront and other beachfront communities. Since this community is positioned on the coast of the sea, so the homes here continuously struggle with humidity and erosion that can impact the interior & exterior of these homes. There is no doubt that homes in Emaar Beachfront are made from the best construction quality but your home appliances need extra care and spend a little extra on maintenance costs. 

Struggle With Traffic

Another big disadvantage of living in Emaar Beachfront is that you have to struggle with high traffic during peak hours. Yes, it is true that the Emaar Beachfront community have its separate entrance and is restricted to outsiders. But, just outside the community gate, there are many popular spots, the best restaurants and many entertainment zones which attract a large number of tourists and visitors. So, keep this point also in mind. 

Conclusion

So, these are the possible pros & cons of living in Emaar Beachfront in Dubai. Living in Emaar Beachfront homes truly presents an exclusive living experience, whether it be apartments or villas and is without any doubt a perfect real estate investment in Dubai. If you are looking for the best waterfront community in Dubai, do check out Emaar Beachfront.
