Real Estate CRM Introduction

Before briefing you in detail, I would like to expand the abbreviation of CRM which stands for Customer Relationship Management. It's technology or you can say software and the term denotes, managing all your company’s relationships and interactions with customers and potential customers.

The emergence of advanced technology has captured the business world and real estate is no longer untouched or unaware of it. In fact, real estate is the only domain that is highly dependent upon it. Because it helps to provide information or data in facts, generating leads and maintaining a long-term relationship with customers which is the most effective strategy to get success in the business. Real estate CRM software helps to improve sales in various ways, let’s have a close look: -

Database Management- Closely associated with real estate CRM, where you have to manage all the available properties to target prospects. You need to do strategic planning of the property with the help of an automated marketing tool. 

Capturing Lead- After planning and implementing CRM it can track the customer interests on the property and which helps in the lead pipeline management so that sales executives can show the clients their preferences with the help of a pop-up alert or reminder. And when the customer gets such an alert automatically, they visit the site and show their interest which will help to generate a lead. 

Marketing Automation- Marketing plays a crucial role in any business hence to make this step more effective here the software assists with two-way email synchronization and auto communication which helps boost sales.

Proper Documentation – It’s quite an easy part with CRM software. As we all know to keep or maintaining documentation is always tiresome and sometimes it comes with errors. Whereas it is easy to store data or other information and go paperless, soft copies here will save you time and help you keep error-free documentation management. 

Nurturing Leads –The best part is, once the lead is generated in CRM, you can send auto emails, and SMS and can run campaigns on an everyday basis. Also, you can take individual follow-up actions that eventually nurture the lead and makes it ready for sale. 

Mobility – You can access the CRM sitting anywhere. Whether you are in travel, or whether you are out of the office, you can manage your offers, deals, and closing sales from anywhere.

Types of CRM

Generally, CRM application comes in three types such as – Operational, Analytical, and Collaborative with different features and services. 

Operational CRM 

Such CRM works or keeps the information and records the data of customers precisely. Operational Real Estate CRM works in 3 steps which include sales automation, marketing automation, and service automation. The purpose of such CRM is to generate leads, convert them into contacts, and capture each detail so that it can provide the service as per the customer's expectations. 

 Let’s understand these 3 steps in more detail:- 

Sales Automation

In this process, it maintains a standard with the organization to acquire new customers and simultaneously deal with the existing customers. Sales automation’s primary purpose is to make the sales process automatic. Hence, it precisely keeps the information in a systematic format by which it can meet the customer’s needs and provide them with exactly what they are seeking which is a great way to increase sales. In an operational CRM, the sales modules work in this way- lead management, contact management, Quote-to-Order management, and sales forecasting. 

Marketing Automation 

Now comes Marketing automation whose primary purpose is to find the best way to reach potential customers. In this step, it manages the campaign, which is the most effective way to market your product via effective channels like emails, phone calls, ads on social media, face-to-face meetings, etc.  

Service Automation

In this process, it offers the best quality services to the customers and helps to build a strong relationship with them.  Here the process works as issue management where they fix customer’s problems, customer call management to handle incoming as well as outgoing calls, etc. 

Analytical CRM

When it comes to management, marketing, sales, and providing customer service the Analytical CRM application stands out as the best option too. Data analysis is the prime function of such a type of CRM application. Hence it collects customer data coming from different touch points which helps an organization understand its customer’s search intent. 

By this management will be able to understand and take better decisions, and marketing executives can run campaigns effectively to target the customers which will improve sales. Moreover, such an effective or you can say planned method will surely build a strong customer relationship.  

Collaborative CRM

Alternatively known as strategic CRM which shares customers’ information with various business units like the sales team, marketing team, technical and support team.  For instance, feedback from a support team is useful for the marketing team to approach the customer with a specific product. 

In the real world, each business unit works independently in a group and rarely shares any information or customer data with other teams, they keep the records to themselves which causes business losses. 

Whereas in Collaborative CRM each business unit must share the data and feedback with a team helps to unite all groups to aim towards one goal. It not only helps you to have a clear understanding of the customer you are serving but it will help increase sales value.

Why do realtors need a CRM?

Almost half of the job is done by CRM, it’s the prime source of leads required in a real estate business.  It helps keep all data of clients, and their interest in home buying and allows the relators to manage deals and transactions.  

What percentage of realtors use a CRM?

CRM software helps real estate agents to stay organized and successfully manage the customer relationship management between property buyers and sellers. To run the business successfully nearly 21% of realtors use CRM in the real estate business.

What are the 3 Primary Goals of the CRM Process?

Generally, CRM is used to manage your customer relationship with the company. Maintains the record of the customer. Assist them by solving their query or information they want to know. Further, send an automated message or notify them, take follow up which later helps to generate a lead. Here are the 3 primary goals of CRM given below:-

  • Capture new leads and move them through the sales process
  • Support and manage relationships with the customers to maximize their lifetime value to the company 
  • Boost sales productivity and helps to grow business 

What are the 4 phases in CRM?

CRM always works in phases like mentioned below:-

1.    Marketing – by running marketing campaigns such as emails and advertisements you can create a database for accounts and contacts which helps to capture leads by scrutinizing the gathered customer data. 
2.    Sales – In this stage, you have to give a primary focus on leads. 
3.    Product – here the prime focus is the product, give every authentic detail about it such as price, vendor and descriptions 
4.    Support – assist your customer by resolving their issues and providing customer support
