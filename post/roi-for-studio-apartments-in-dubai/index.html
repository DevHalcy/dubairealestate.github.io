What Is A Studio Apartment?

A studio apartment or single room dwelling place or studio flat consists of one big room which contains living, dining and bedroom or in other words, it is a self contained space that combines everything in a single room with a separate bathroom.

BEST FOR?

Investors- As it ensures high rental yields and ROI between 5 -8.4%

Home buyer- From 2-3 members it would be the best fit where its compact design will give you a comfortable space to live in, and for a constrained budget, this would be the cherry on the top.

One of the exotic communities in Dubai that contains the largest manmade island and is an eye catchy landmark where living is a blessing. Palm Jumeirah comes with a collection of townhouses, hotels, apartments and villas. World-class amenities and aesthetic views are the main pillars that make the living idea more special and content. And then monorail connects the different areas in the Palm. Its 560 hectares area is divided into 3 beautiful segments- The Trunk, Crescent and Fronds.
