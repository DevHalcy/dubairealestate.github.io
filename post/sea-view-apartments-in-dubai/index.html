Popular Areas With Sea View Apartments In Dubai

Looking for sea view apartments in Dubai with a picturesque view of the Persian Gulf? We have compiled a list of the best areas with sea view apartments, so you can easily choose the best apartment.

Sea view apartments are purposely done to bring a serene yet tranquil aura and nature close to you. It gives perfect relaxation , and fills with a positive vibe and calm surroundings. Dubai is quite famous for serving such a kind of lifestyle as you know the Arabian Gulf is close to the city. So here I am going to tell you the most popular areas with sea view apartments in Dubai that are highly desired by the Arabs as well as immigrants.

PALM JUMEIRAH

One of the most famous areas not in only Dubai but known in the entire world for its first-ever artificial island. Also, considered the eighth wonder and the world’s most eye-catching landmarks. This island is developed in the shape of a Palm tree which makes it unique among the other Seven Wonders of the World. 

Palm Jumeirah is the major attraction of Dubai and it receives higher tourist visitation for its panoramic view of the sea. It is a popular residential as well as recreational place in Dubai and that makes the island livelier. Myriads of amenities are waiting here to let you explore the luxury Dubai. You can enjoy culinary delights in these island restaurants. For adventurers, the sight has a number of interesting activities. 

For a perfect beach lifestyle, Palm Jumeriah is the best destination. It offers a cozy lifestyle around you and keeps all the entertaining amenities to let you relish the best part of your life.  You can see a variety of properties on this island including apartments, palatial mansions to upscale flats. 

Apartment Prices In Palm Jumeirah

  • A 1 BR apartment in Palm Jumeirah starts with AED 137K
  • If you are single you can get a studio apartment in rent too in AED 87K
  • If you are investing in 2-bed flats in Dubai then you can expect AED 175 K for rent 
  • For a sea view apartment it will cost you AED 252 K annually 

Dubai Marina

Another famous destination for sea view apartments in Dubai. Here you can expect luxury segments of apartments where you will enjoy not only the home but the mesmerizing water view. 

The localities of Dubai Marina are brimmed with unending facilities. It offers you the best connectivity to other parts of Dubai also, and you will enjoy ultra-modern civic amenities close to this it. Properties from various categories are available here like studio apartments and lavish penthouses. 

Apartment Prices In Dubai Marina 

  • A studio apartment in rent will cost AED 56 per annum
  • A 1 BR average cost will be AED 84 k per year 
  • A 2 BR rent apartment will cost AED 127 K
  • A 3 bedroom will cost AED 194K yearly

DUBAI CREEK HARBOUR

One of the most popular waterfront destinations in Dubai where the community is beautifully developed with luxurious apartments and Villas. The lifestyle in this community is just amazing and the area holds outstanding neighborhoods, and the fee holds properties. 

It is just 15 minutes from Dubai downtown which is the home to the tallest Burj Khalifa hence you can enjoy the waterfront as well as the iconic tower view right from your apartment. Also, you will get various choices of residence in studios and chillers-free apartments. 

Apartment Price In Dubai Creek Harbour

  • 1-bed apartment in  Dubai Creek Harbour  for rent will cost AED 75,000 per year
  • 2 bedroom apartment for rent will cost AED 110.000 per year  
  • Chillers-free apartment for rent will cost AED 85,000 per year 

Al SUFOUH

On the western coast of Dubai Al Sufouh is gleaning more attention for serving outstanding sea view apartments.  Seamlessly connected to the trunk of the Palm Jumeirah, moreover, it is home to a wild wadi waterpark (a major attraction for all age groups). The community is surrounded by up-to-scale amenities. It’s the best place for family and if you are seeking your choice then a 3 BR will be the best option for you in Al Sufouh. 

Apartments Price in Al Sufouh 

  • A rental studio apartment will cost you roughly AED 51 K annually 
  • A 1 BR sea view apartment in Al Sufouh will cost AED 72K
  • A 2 BR flat on rent will cost AED 78 K

JUMEIRAH BEACH RESIDENCE

JBR in Dubai is especially famous for its beachfront living and this community offers sea view apartments. So, for the beach lover, this is another popular destination in Dubai. The area is home to approx. 35 residences and outstanding facilities. You can unwind yourself in top-class amenities developed on JBR beach like Jet Ski (the best spot to relax in Dubai).

Apartments Prices In Jumeirah Beach Residence

  • A 1 BR apartment in JBR rental price is AED 101K
  • A 2 BR waterfront apartment can give a you rental yield of AED 143K annually
  • A 3 BR waterfront rent apartment will cost you AED 171K

Bottom Line

Dubai is indeed the best city for those who want to go the extra mile to experience an extraordinary lifestyle between wonders, luxury, and adventure. The best part is the lifestyle close to the sea which only one can experience in an apartment and for that, there are multiple areas with their renowned specialty. The minimum rental apartment price near a seafront will cost you up to AED 51 K in all the above-mentioned locations. Thus, if you are preferring waterfront living then you can shortlist the one according to your preferences.
