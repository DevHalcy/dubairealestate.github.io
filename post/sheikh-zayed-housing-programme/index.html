Assistance Provided By Sheikh Zayed Housing Programme?

Not just this, but with complete assistance it offers housing aid to grant non refundable financial, then financial loans to improve your housing experience or even for a house in ministry built complexes and lastly, utility housing is for rent and other expenses such as sewage service, water & electricity, natural gas and more.

And in case you want more information on this programme, then you can make a call on 800 6634 or else can visit their office Sunday-Thursday (8 am- 2 pm).

Time To Apply For Housing Assistance, But How?

So for this you need to arrange the following documents and ensure that they all are valid:-

  • A copy of the family book
  • Passport and identity card
  • Real estate property certificate issued by the competent authority 
  • Commercial licenses certificated issued by the same authority
  • Proof of your income where you can submit a salary certificate, financial aid certificate or proof that the applicant has no income

And in case a divorced woman wants to apply should get a certificate issued by the competent court providing her custody of her children.

  • Non marriage certificate 
  • Certificate showing the retirement pension on behalf of her deceased husband

And now the ministry may train the applicant to submit any information, or documents within a time not exceeding 90 days from the date of the request, otherwise, it may not be valid. Apart from this, a housing assistance application shall be refused if all documents are not attached or valid, so the correctness of the data is ensured by the ministry, and if the wrong data is given intentionally, then the housing assistance will be withdrawn. 

  • Fill out and submit the application form
  • Announcement of the name of the candidate for housing assistance
  • Bring the documents of the applicant 
  • Cross check every submitted document
  • Program board approval
  • Announcing the final approvals with the help of the media

And then simply with the help of an official website you can follow up the application by entering the user name and password.

Then if you are concerned about the value of the loan, then the value of the housing assistance maximum limit for a grant or loan is 800,000 dirhams, and for assistance for governmental housing or housing loan within a residential complex depending on the cost of the establishment of the house and it should not exceed 800,000 dirhams.

The monthly income limit of the grand recipient applies to a person whose average monthly income is less than 15,000 dirhams. And note, The Council of Ministers may change the value of the housing assistance and the monthly income for the recipient of the financial grant, and this is entirely based on the economic conditions, prices of goods & services, inflation rates and proposal by the minister. 

Now, what if you want to cancel your application? So the documents that are required are the family book along with filling out the cancellation request.

Now let us close this by adding up some important FAQs on Sheikh Zayed Housing Programme:- 

Q- What is required after issuing a housing assistance decision?

Apply the requirement of opening the implementation file.

Q- What are the reasons for the delay in issuing the approval decision?

The government is already working on its current plan to allocate 1,726 housing units and to make the process easier and simpler the government is determining the need of an individual by scaling the groups on the basis of 3 main aspects- Social, financial and residential aspect. 

Q- How can the data of the submitted housing assistance application be updated?

With the help of the website or Ministry’s services application, the housing support applicant can be updated in the real time.

Q- Can Gulf citizens like Saudi apply for housing Sheikh Zayed?

The applicant must be a citizen that is the first and foremost criteria to keep in mind, and rest there are different aspects to watch.

Q- What are the working hours of this program?

8:00 am to 14:00 pm from Sunday to Thursday and the call center timings are 7:30 am to 7:30 pm from Sunday to five.

Q- What is meant by initial and final approval?

The name of the applicant in the general list based on the points system in preparation to obtain grant or loan, and for final approval issuance of the decision for granting of the user to either the grant or the loan.

Q- How many homes will be handed to citizens by 2022 under Sheikh Zayed Housing Programme?

Nearly 10,000 new homes will be delivered by 2022 to the citizens connected to Sheikh Zayed Housing Programme. And by 2026 it will build 15,800 homes for nationals in Dubai

Q- What types of properties are there in Sheikh Zayed Housing Programme?

Recently, the ministry identified 100 housing design models which blend in every type of need and desire. And they have completed 36,000 homes for Emiratis.
