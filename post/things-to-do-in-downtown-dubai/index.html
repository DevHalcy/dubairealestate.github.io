Best Things To Do In Downtown Dubai Tips You Will Read This Year

Would you like to enjoy the dancing fountain in Downtown Dubai or do you wish to explore the world’s tallest tower Burj Khalifa? Whatever your wish, I bet you will enjoy it more, so here I have brought you the best things to do in Downtown Dubai.

This lifestyle destination in Dubai receives higher tourist visitation, yet it carries the famous world’s wonders within. Caught attention from all over the world – Yes, I am talking about Downtown Dubai. Located in the heart of Dubai, this major place is ruling over everyone’s mind. That’s right!
 
Downtown Dubai is showcasing a perfect blend of traditional and modern architecture, introducing lifestyle and meeting fun, entertainment, activities and many more at a single destination. Surrounded by the world’s tallest Burj Khalifa and the world’s largest shopping mall – The Dubai Mall it holds all sumptuous experiences just for you only!
 
So, if the place is calling you and you are ready for a tour then do not miss these best places to visit in Downtown Dubai.

Explore World’s Largest Dubai Mall

If you are in Dubai and that too in the Downtown area. Do not forget to explore the largest shopping mall in UAE. Yes, here I am talking about The Dubai Mall! If I say it is the largest shopping destination which has both entertainment and leisure amenities, would you believe it? I guess you would not believe my words. But let me tell you that this is the only place where you will get fun. 

Adjacent to the world’s tallest building Burj Khalifa is getting more attention and more visitors. Dubai Mall has 1200 retail stores, and more than 200 stores are food & beverage outlets. 

Dubai Mall has unlimited entertainment options from a concert arena to a private function area where you can host your party. A walk-through aquarium, go-kart circuit, flight stimulation and huge indoor ice rink are there in the mall. So, all in all, it’s a perfect destination for visitors to explore. 

Major Attractions In Dubai Mall:

Dubai Aquarium, explore the underwater marine life where more than 33,000 sea creatures occupied 10 million litters of water in the tank.  

Best Time To Visit Dubai Mall:

Between the month of December - February, you will experience something different because this is the time when the Dubai Shopping Festivals begins. 

Dubai Mall Location:

Financial Center Street, Along Sheikh Zayed Road, Next to Burj Khalifa – Dubai-United Arab Emirates.

Dubai Fountain – World’s Largest Choreographed Fountain

Dubai fountain is another beautiful attraction in the Downtown Dubai area. World’s largest choreographed fountains, that’s right! The best thing about the fountain is, it is designed in a brilliant way with a combination of lights and different kinds of music such as Arabic, classical and International contemporary. So, when the music starts to play simultaneously the fountain sprays jets of illuminated water up to 500 feet and the view is just breathtaking. 

It feels like the fountain got alive with the music and by dancing it is presenting its gratitude. This musical performance of the dancing fountain is amazing. The best part, is when it comes to life after every 30 minutes. For a while, you will forget yourself and get immersed in the show.  Won’t you believe me? You can check it out by yourself!

Best Time To Visit Dubai Fountain

After 6 P.M.

Dubai Fountain Location

Sheikh Mohammad Bin Rashid Blvd, Downtown, Dubai, UAE

Dubai Opera – A Multi-format Theatre With 2000 Seats

Dubai Opera hosts a broad variety of internationally recognised performers throughout the year. This cultural and art venue is located in the rich area of Downtown Dubai where almost performances are organised every year by the organizer. This 2000-seats multi-format theatre frequently receives international performances including ballet, orchestras, and pop. It also offers local singers a chance to perform in this finest destination. Dubai Opera has its own official website too.

Best Time To Visit Dubai Opera

After the evening onwards

Dubai Opera Location

Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Blvd, Dubai, UAE

Dubai Water Canal – Sustainable & Environmental Project

Dubai Water Canal is the most innovative sustainable and environmental project. The project is stretched from Old Dubai to Arabian Gulf and is the most amazing development in Dubai. Stunningly designed with other side attractions including a 3 km running track, 12 km cycling track and a 6.4 km artificial waterfall. 

The Canal has 5 pedestrian bridges that allow visitors to walk and enjoy the water as well as the waterfall view while walking in the fresh air. That indicates the surroundings near Dubai Canal is more pleasing for pedestrian. Your morning, as well as an evening walk, is going to be more energetic with such motivating views around. 

Best Time To Visit Dubai Water Canal

You can go after 6 PM

Dubai Water Canal Location

Jumeirah- Jumeirah 2, Dubai,UAE

Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo – Explore Marine Life

For family, exploring under Dubai Aquarium & underwater zoo with your kids is one of the best places to visit in Downtown Dubai. As I had given a small glimpse about this most visited place in the gold city. This family-friendly destination is located in Downtown Dubai it is the best place to have fun with your kids. Dubai Aquarium is one of the world’s largest indoor aquariums where visitors can explore underwater life or you can say marine life. It’s going to be a thrilling tour of numerous underwater creatures. 

This 48-meter Dubai Aquarium tunnel will make your tour more memorable; it provides you with 270-degree views of this giant aquarium. Visitors can able to see crabs, sea horses, jelly, dolphins, seaweeds and many more marine creatures and feels like for a while you are in their world. They are watching you and you are playing with them is just an amazing experience. 

Best Time To Visit Dubai Aquarium & underwater zoo

10:00 AM – 10:00 PM (Sun-Wed) and 10:00 AM – 10:12:00 (Thu-Sat)

Dubai Aquarium & underwater zoo Location

Unit SF -115-Downtown Dubai, Dubai, UAE

KidZania – Let Your Kids Learn More!

For kids, this would be the best place and I am sure they would love it. KidZania is the place where they learn the first step to socialization and understand what they want to become in the future. Its prime aim is to teach kids and teenagers between four to six how to grow in the real world by playing through certain job roles. 

In KidZania they feel free with their peer group by playing an act on certain jobs like doctor, firefighter, police officer, etc and by participating in this act they get to understand and simulate the real world. That means this activity helps them to understand the certain job role they played. They are also rewarded and sponsored by branded market leaders of both multinationals and locals. 

KidZania Age limit

up to 14 years old

Number of job activities teaches

40 role-playing activity

Relax at Burj Park

Burj Park is one of the popular leisure destinations, with ample greenery, it reflects healthy vibes. The atmosphere of this area is purposely done to let you enjoy nature. Specifically, in winter with food truck festivals, art soirees and other events this area comes to life and at that time you can enjoy the place. 

Burj Park is adjacent to the Dubai Dancing fountain so you can enjoy the show time while enjoying the illuminated lights in different colors playing in the park. It’s a quite relaxing place to walk and the best thing to do in the downtown area.  Although Burj park is used as the venue that organised various events still you can go for a walk, there are also a running track in the park where you can warm up yourself every day. 

Best Time To Visit Burj Park

The park is open at all times but if you just want to relax and enjoy the dancing fountain show then after sunset between 4-6 is the best time to go.

Burj Park Location

Virgin Dubai Mall, Downtown Dubai, UAE

Visit Dubai Frame – The Futuristic Landmark

Another major attraction in Downtown Dubai- Dubai Frame, is a futuristic landmark that perfectly represents the old and new Dubai. Its height is nearly 500 feet and is considered the world’s largest picture frame. Tourists from various places frequently visit this place to see Dubai Frame. The structure is so beautifully done that it can cover the entire iconic Burj Khalifa in the frame. 

Best Time To Visit Dubai Frame

In the Morning and During the Sunset

Dubai Frame Location

Zabeel Park Jogging Track- Za’abeel- Al Kifaf- Dubai, UAE

Get 360 Degree View of Dubai atop Burj Khalifa

The world’s iconic tower Burj Khalifa is a wonder itself in Dubai. This skyscraper building is 828 meters in height with 200+ storeys. This masterpiece has the world’s longest escalator. From the top of the tower, you can enjoy a 360 degrees view of the entire Dubai City.  This mixed-used tower is comprised of residential, commercial and hospitality ventures.  Burj Khalifa is famous all over the world for broke numerous records like the world’s tallest free-standing structure, the world’s highest occupied floor, and the world’s highest outdoor observation deck. Visitors from across the world visit this place to see this wonder.

Best Time To Visit Burj Khalifa

9: 30 pm to 3:30 pm – 6:30 pm -until closing 

Burj Khalifa Ticket Price

There are different prices for kids and adults. approx. 42 USD is the maximum price. AED 154 for 12 years+, AED119 for 4-12 years, for infants (below 4 years) it’s free.

Burj Khalifa Location

Emaar Boulevard, Downtown Dubai, Mohammad Bin Rashid Boulevard, 1, UAE

Shopping Malls In Downtown Dubai

For shopaholics, Downtown Dubai is the best place with different popular malls, where you can experience shopping and can shop till you drop. So, you all know about The Dubai Mall- the largest one in the world, Right? But do you know other malls located in Downtown Dubai? Well, if you don’t know, you can explore Dubai Festival City Mall, Dubai Outlet Mall, City Centre Deira, Dubai Marina Mall, Ibn Battuta Mall (the largest themed mall in the world), Gallery one, Souk Al Bahar, Galeries  Lafayette, Gallery One Dubai Mall, Kinokuniya Book Store, etc.

Let’s have a short glimpse of some malls where you can unwind yourself better: - 

(1) Dubai Festival City Mall

We all know shopping is always associated with buying a varied collection of branded items. It can be apparel, household stuff and many more. But in Dubai Festival City Mall it is an experience that’ll almost surprise you. There are plenty of things which offer international brands, furniture stores etc.

Along with this, Dubai Festival City Mall provides a business and entertainment centre, located at Dubai Creek. For business stays and corporate conferences viewing this is the best place. 

Best Time To Visit Dubai Festival City Mall

10 AM -10 PM (Sun-Wed), 10 AM – 12 midnight ( Thus- Sat)

Dubai Festival City Mall Location

Dubai Creek, Dubai, UAE

(2) Ibn Battuta Mall

Dubai’s one of the largest themed malls Ibn Battuta Mall has 270 shops, 45,000 parking spaces, 50 restaurants, and a 21- screen cinema. Nestled over Sheikh Zayed Road it is letting shoppers explore the world’s best-themed malls. 

Designed by the Moroccan explorer Ibn Battuta, who has travelled to six different countries- Tunisia, Egypt, India, Andalusia, China and Persia hence the mall is designed into six courts which belong to these six countries where you can explore the art and culture of six different countries. 

Best time to visit Ibn Battuta Mall:  10 AM- 10 PM ( Sat-Wed), 10 AM – 10 PM (Thus-Fri)

Highlights of the Mall: Novo Cinema, large Carrefour Supermarket, six different courts theme is inspired by six different countries.

Location: Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai, UAE

(3) Galeries Lafayette

This is the first franchise, a shopping mall located in the Downtown area is another place where you can explore fashion and home collections on various international brands. You can get all the luxury accessibility, fashion brands, the latest trends in interior design and much more. 

Exploring this mall will meet your every need. Several branded collections in this mall surely satisfy your taste in fashion. The malls offer a range of products for women, men and kids’ fashion. So definitely shopping at this mall will be the best experience for your entire family. And suppose you get tired of shopping you can revive yourself in The Lafayette Gourmet which provides delectable cuisine and several other food products. A restaurant name- Red Box located in this mall is good at serving French cuisine, you can try that. 

Location: Emaar Blvd, Dubai Mall

Quick Tips To Visit Downtown Dubai

  • If you want to explore the city then the metro is the most convenient yet inexpensive option.
  • Once you arrived at the airport, ask for a free SIM card which help you to access maps and other websites important at the time of need.
  • Although you can’t afford a beach resort, don’t get panic. Other free public beaches out there like Kite Beach and JBR Beach offer sun loungers, showers, and eateries.
  • Plan your trip to Dubai between November through March, this is the best time as the temperature is approx. 30 degree Celsius.

FAQs

Q- What is downtown Dubai known for?

Downtown is a posh area in Dubai and is known as the city’s largest landmark. Spanned over 2 Square Kilometres area this place is studded with numerous world-famous attractions such as Burj Khalifa, The Dubai Mall, and Dubai Fountain,etc.

Q- Can you walk around downtown Dubai?

Yes, you can. There are many pedestrian paths and walking routes in Dubai Marina and Jumeirah Lakes Towers. Although Downtown areas have not that much of tourists destination still you can explore some places on foot. 

Q- Is Downtown Dubai a good area?

Undoubtedly, the Downtown area is located in the heart of Dubai and the place is quite popular and known as the posh area with an outstanding lifestyle. Also, you can easily discover many things to do in this area. For, fun and entertainment Downtown Dubai is the best place with lots of choices. 

Q- How do you get around Downtown Dubai?

Well, that’s not even hard to get around Downtown Dubai. You can discover it by taxi and private car and yes, you can use the Dubai Metro facilities which are quite convenient. Once you exit from the airport to the city centre you will get Dubai’s Red Metro Line close to Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 from where you can easily get to Dubai Downtown.

Q- How far is Downtown Dubai from Jumeirah Beach?

Jumeirah Beach is just 5 KM away from Downtown Dubai.

Q- Which are the best places to visit in Downtown Dubai?

Burj Khalifa, Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, The Dubai Fountain, The Dubai Mall, etc are the best places to visit in Downtown Dubai.

Q- How many days are enough to explore Downtown Dubai?

Well, 7 days is enough to explore Downtown Dubai but It entirely depends on you. If your budget allows you to discover Downtown Dubai more closely you can extend your stay. 

Q- Where to stay in Downtown Dubai?

If you are planning for a vacation in Downtown Dubai and searching for the best place to stay then there are many luxury hotels are available in this area. The Oberoi, Le Royal Meridien Beach Resort, Five Downtown, etc.
