Top 10 Places for Outdoor Brunches in Dubai

There's nothing quite like enjoying a delicious brunch outdoors in Dubai. The city has some amazing places to enjoy a leisurely meal in the sun.

If you're looking for the best places to enjoy an outdoor brunch in Dubai, you've come to the right place! Here are our top 10 picks for the best spots to enjoy a sunny meal with friends or family.

Atelier M

In Dubai, Atelier M offers a Dreamy breakfast with a view of the marina. The food is a result of blending several different cuisines with a heavy French flavor.

Here, your order is delivered in a very considerate manner. The furnishings and environment are carefully created to offer you a luxury and remarkable experience.

This will be a perfect place for your Saturday brunch. The dishes that you must try when you visit here are Truffle Risotto, Oysters, and Mashed Potatoes. 

Location – Pier 7, 7th floor, Marina Mall, Dubai Marina

Brunch Timing – 2 pm to 5 pm on Saturdays 
 

The World Eatery

A perfect Sunday brunch option in Dubai. This eatery is named for its wide brunch menu, which offers cuisine from around the globe.

In one of Palm Jumeirah's top eateries, you may experience a distinctive brunch flavor here.

The clothing is casual, the beverages are free-flowing, the service is warm, and the cuisine is top-notch, so you can expect a very enjoyable brunch experience.

The World Eatery is one of the best places that serve the best and most reasonably priced brunches in all of Dubai.

Location – East Crescent road, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai

Brunch Timing – 12:30 pm to 3 pm on Sundays
 

Belgian Café

This café screams "Welcome to Belgium" from every direction you look.

You will have the impression that you have flown in on the first trip once you see the incredible wooden architecture used to construct the building.

This café is known for serving some of the city's most mouth-watering burgers and Belgian fries.

As a suitable accompaniment to your breakfast there, we strongly suggest that you try some of their cocktails.

If you're yearning for a Saturday brunch, then this would be the best spot in Jumeirah Dubai to have an evening brunch.

Location – Souk Madinat Jumeirah, Dubai

Brunch Timing – 2 pm to 5 pm on Saturdays 

Boca

The exquisite flavors of Mediterranean food are brought to you at Boca, a restaurant that has won several awards.

The level of care for detailed work shown here is unrivaled; as soon as you walk in, you will be pleased by the contrasts and the design of the modern-chic space.

We promise that every moment you spend here will be infused with warm and enjoyable feelings.

It's undoubtedly one of Dubai's best-kept brunch secrets, and Spanish cuisine is the main influence on the menu.

For vegan people, Boca also provides a unique brunch menu.

Location – Gate Village 6, Dubai International Financial Centre

Brunch Timing – 1 pm to 4 pm on Saturdays 
 

Hell’s Kitchen

You've undoubtedly heard Hell's Kitchen if you're a foodie, the kitchen of Gordon Ramsay.

We're speaking about experiencing the cuisine from one of the world's most well-known culinary programs. One of the best brunch options in Dubai for families is Hell's Kitchen.

Hell's Kitchen offers unique cuisine where you may order and savor the delectable flavor of Gordon Ramsay's international cuisines.

It is, without a doubt, the greatest Friday brunch in all of Dubai.

Location – Palace Tower, Caesar Palace, Bluewaters Island, Dubai.

Brunch Timing– 12:30 pm to 3:30 pm on Fridays 

La Cantine Du Faubourg

It is a French-influenced restaurant. There is nothing like it anywhere else; the air is filled with elegance, comfort, and romance.

The decor, music, and serving dishes are all elements that come together to create a beautiful work of art.

This place offers signature plates with one-of-a-kind dressings that add taste to your meal.

You can enjoy the nicest outdoor brunch here on the lovely terrace of La Cantine du Faubourg.

Location –105 Jumeirah Emirates Towers Hotel, Trade Centre, Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai

Brunch Timing – 12 pm to 5 pm on Saturdays and Sundays

COYA

The magnificent décor of COYA will definitely attract your eye.

One of the top brunch venues in Dubai, the restaurant is influenced by Latin American culture in every way.

Everything from the Peruvian food to the hand-carved tables and chairs to the unique serving dishes on display here is a cut above the rest.

You can count on getting more out of your Friday Brunch in Dubai.

Location – Restaurant Village, Four Seasons Resort Jumeirah Beach Road, Jumeirah 2, Dubai

Brunch Timing – 12:30 pm to 4 pm Fridays 
 

NOBU

Do you have an appetite for Japanese delicacies? You can count on Nobu to provide the finest selections for a top-notch Saturday brunch in Dubai.

One of the greatest sushi brunch meals in Dubai can be found on the menu, which is a delightful combination of Peruvian and Japanese cuisines.

It's easy to feel relaxed and pampered in the plush atmosphere created by the dim lighting and the kind staff.

The brunch offerings are genuine and one-of-a-kind.

Because it enhances your meal with only the finest ingredients, Nobu's brunch will be a memorable experience.

Location –The Avenues, Atlantis, The Palm Jumeirah, Dubai

Brunch Timing –12 pm to 3:30 pmon Saturdays 
 

Ce La Vi Dubai

Ce La Vi, a restaurant and lounge with a rooftop pool and stunning views of the Dubai cityscape, has debuted its signature brunch.

This eatery has one of the most interesting brunch bargain menus in all of Dubai and will definitely cater to your every need.

There's something special about this spot, whether it's the sounds or the scenery.

The weekend is the best time to do this. If you're traveling to Dubai with the kids, you must eat at Ce La Vi since it's among the city's greatest restaurants.

Location –Sky View Hotel, Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Blvd, Downtown Dubai

Brunch Timing – 12:30 pm to 4 pm on Saturdays
 

Great British Restaurant

Prepare yourself with a sense of longing if you decide to visit this location.

You'll have a wonderful time at a 90s-themed brunch, where the music, design, and atmosphere will make you feel warm and fuzzy inside.

This brunch is among the cheapest in Dubai. The menu features meals with a British culinary influence.

Your kids will love the Duksey Kids Club included in your stay. It's Saturday night, so it's time to party at a one-of-a-kind Dubai brunch restaurant.

Location –Dukes in Royal Hideaway hotel, The Palm Jumeirah,  Dubai

Brunch Timing – 1 pm to 4 pm on Saturdays

Conclusion

So, if you're looking for a great place to enjoy an outdoor brunch in Dubai with your loved ones, then no more waiting.Check out these amazing options for your weekend brunches in Dubai.
