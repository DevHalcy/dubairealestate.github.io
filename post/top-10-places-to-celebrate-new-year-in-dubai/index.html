Top 10 Places to Celebrate New Year’s Eve in Dubai

If you want to celebrate New Year’s Eve in style, then you need to head to Dubai. This city knows how to party in style, and you’ll be able to enjoy all the luxury and glamour that Dubai is known for.

From chic hotels and restaurants to stunning fireworks displays, there’s something for everyone to celebrate New Year in Dubai. No New Year’s Eve celebration in Dubai would be complete without a stunning fireworks display. You’ll be able to enjoy a spectacular show as you ring in the New Year, and you’ll have memories that will last a lifetime. Let’s look at the top 10 places where you can celebrate New Year in Dubai.

BurjKhalifa

Burj Khalifa would be the number one option when you want to extravagantly celebrate New Year in Dubai. When it comes to New Year's Eve celebrations, the Burj Khalifa is the unequivocal winner. The world's highest building has the best and most extravagant New Year's Eve celebration. Get there early to escape the throng and get a seat with a good view. One of the amazing parts of watching the New Year's Eve fireworks from this location is that the Burj Khalifa tower transforms into a gigantic LED screen, displaying a laser and light show to complement the fireworks.

The best time to visit is 11:58 PM

Al Seef

Visit Al Seef, a traditional Dubai neighbourhood that is right on the Dubai Creek. Ancient buildings and traditional dhows provide for a breathtaking backdrop. A perfect way to celebrate New Year in Dubai with a mesmerising traditional touch. This charming area displays the city's great past and bright future by blending traditional beauty with contemporary architecture and design.A cultural tour of the pedestrian-friendly historical neighbourhood of Al Fahidi , accompanied by a meander around Al Seef's magnificent walkways, fully operational marina, eating boulevard, and events plaza, is the finest way to get a feel for the area.

Best time to visit: 9:30 PM
 

Ain Dubai

The stunning cityscape of Dubai can be seen from Ain Dubai on Bluewaters Island, making it the perfect place to celebrate New Year in Dubai. From the top of the world’s biggest and tallest observation wheel, you can see fireworks going off all around Dubai in places like the Burj Khalifa (Downtown Dubai) to the Palm in Atlantis. Discover the thrilling sounds and sights of the neighborhood early in the evening; they are complemented by the various food choices along the beach and the family-friendly activities available at Madame Tussauds.

Best time to visit is 7 PM to 9:30 PM
 

Atlantis The Palm

This famous Dubai hotel hosts a spectacular event to welcome in the New Year. Make reservations at one of Atlantis, The Palm's acclaimed dining establishments or buzzing nightclubs, or enjoy a night of dazzle and glamour at the hotel's New Year's Eve gala.If you want celebrate New Year in Dubai in a more traditional way, then you can head to one of Dubai’s many restaurants. There are numerous restaurants that offer special New Year’s Eve menus, and you’ll be able to find something to suit your taste.Try a variety of well-known cafes and eateries endorsed by celebrities, like Hakkasan and Bread Street Kitchen of Gordon Ramsay. 

Best time to visit: 9 PM
 

Sparkling Scenes from Yacht

From the waters of the Arabian Gulf, one may get the best view of Dubai's coastline. Book a fantastic yachting event with one of the most well-known corporations, such as Seven Yachts, Lotus Mega Yacht, or Xclusive, for New Year's Eve and give yourself the opportunity to try something new and exciting. A perfectly lavish and cosy way to celebrate New Year in Dubai. You have the option of requesting a personalised route that takes in the most breathtaking panoramas as well as midnight fireworks, or you can go with one of their one-of-a-kind evening packages that includes delectable treats as well as constant entertainment.

Best time to visit: Evening 

Expo 2023 Dubai

Expo 2023 Dubai is an once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to celebrate New Year in Dubai with a diverse range of people from all around the world. You will get a vast range of world-class food, live entertainment, and amazing midnight feast accompanies by spectacular light and firework shows. It is world’s one of the greatest New Year Eve’s show that you will witness in Dubai. There will be celebrations in honour of the global audience that will be there to exchange new perspectives. In my opinion, it is one of the greatest family-friendly holiday spots in all of Dubai.

Best time to visit: 9 PM
 

La Mer

Families that are interested in combining New Year's Eve festivities with activities in the great outdoors will find that La Mer is a wonderful option. Families will have a great time bouncing over HawaHawa, which is an outdoor trampoline park near La Mer, or sliding and slipping the entire day at Laguna, which is one of the best water parks in the city. A perfect place for families to celebrate New Year in Dubai. Because there are coastal homes, day rooms, and a variety of watersports available, it is recommended to come early so that you may enjoy the complete day.

Best time to visit: Whenever you want to celebrate!

The Beach

The Beach is a seaside resort in Dubai Marina that welcomes guests from all over the world. It is situated just next to the Jumeirah Beach Residences in Dubai Marina.You can celebrate New Year in Dubai on the beaches, in the meantime the kids will enjoy the fun springy water park, or you can book a seat at top hotspot dining options before the night will be filled with captivating fireworks. Both of these activities can be done before the colorful fireworks start. You also have the option of catching a marine taxi at the Marina Mall Station in order to see the event from the perspective of the water.

Best time to visit: Evening 

Aura Skypool

Dubai's premier outdoor hotspot Aura Skypool, and the world's 1st tallest 360-degree infinity pool, will hold a New Year's celebration in the sky on New Year's Eve that you must attend. If you are in the area, you should make plans to attend. The best way to celebrate New Year in Dubai would be Aura Skypool if you want to experience cool and frizzy New Year with mesmerising firework shows. An impression of an "oasis in the sky" will be created by the abundance of lively tunes, delectable foods, and the greatest spectacular views of the fireworks performances that will be taking place around the city at the same time. 

Best time to visit: Evening 

Five Star Hotel

If you’re looking for a luxurious way to celebrate New Year in Dubai, then you need to stay at one of Dubai’s many five-star hotels. You’ll be able to enjoy all the amenities and services that these hotels have to offer, and you’ll be able to ring in the New Year in style. You can select from a wide range of options such as the luxurious Park Hyatt Dubai which includes the finest eateries like The Thai Kitchen and NOÉPE. While enjoying a meal at the resort's famous Enigma restaurant, guests of Palazzo Versace may also take in the breathtaking fireworks display along the Jaddaf seashore.

Best time to visit: 9 PM 

Conclusion

So if you’re looking for a truly unforgettable way to celebrate New Year in Dubai, then you need to visit these places. Either with your partner or with your whole family, Dubai has a wide range of options for your every preference. You’ll be able to enjoy all the city has to offer, and you’ll have a New Year’s Eve in Dubai that you’ll never forget.
