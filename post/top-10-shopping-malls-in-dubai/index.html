Top 10 Shopping Malls in Dubai – What you would like to shop there!

Have you ever been to Dubai? If not, you're missing out on some of the best shopping in the world!

The city is home to some of the most lavish and iconic shopping malls, where you can find everything from designer clothes to luxury cars.If you're planning a trip to Dubai, make sure to add these 10 shopping malls to your itinerary:

The Dubai Mall

With over 1,200 stores, the Dubai Mall is one of the largest shopping malls in the world! There's something for everyone here, from high-end designer stores to more affordable brands. You will get everything here, starting from your favorite fashion brand to movie theatres. A wide range of dining outlets is also available in this mall, such as Cheesecake Factory, Laduree, Five Guys, and Magnolia Bakery. 

Timing – For Sunday to Wednesday, it's 10 am to 11 pm. For Thursday to Saturday, it's 10 am to 12 am. 

Location – Downtown Dubai 

Mall of the Emirates

Mall of the Emirates (MOE), which spans over 2 million square feet, has been a popular shopping attraction since it first welcomed customers in Al Barsha in 2005. This mall is home to the world's first indoor ski resort, making it a must-visit for winter sports enthusiasts. In addition to the ski slope, there are over 600 stores to explore, and among them, you will find more than 100 cafes and restaurants. Perfect place for foodie shoppers. There's something for everyone here, from fashion to electronics.

Time – For Sunday to Wednesday, it's 10 am to 10 pm. For Thursday to Saturday, it's 10 am to 12 am.

Location – Al Barsha, Sheikh Zayed Road Interchange Four

Dubai Festival City Mall

This mall is famous for its spectacular views of Dubai's skyline and is also home to over 500 stores. In addition to retail, the mall also offers a range of dining and entertainment options. The main attraction of this mall is the Robinsons' department store, as it provides a wide variety of luxury brands. This mall also has other popular brands like DKNY, GAP, Beverly Hills Polo Club, Mango, Zara, IKEA, and many more. At Sugar Factory, you will get Insta-worthy clicks on their tasty desserts. 

Time – For Sunday to Wednesday, it's 10 am to 10 pm. For Thursday to Saturday, it's 10 am to 12 am.

Location –Dubai Festival City

IBN Battuta Mall

This mall is perfect for those who want to experience a taste of Dubai's rich culture. There are over 200 stores to explore and a range of restaurants and cafes.Named after the famous 14th-century explorer Ibn Battuta, the mall features six different themed courts, each representing a different country he visited. These six courts are Andulasia, Egypt, China,Persia, Tunisia and India. The mall offers guests various F&B establishments, retail stores, and entertainment alternatives.

Time – Every day, 10 am to 10 pm.

Location – Sheikh Zayed Road, Jebel Ali Village

City Centre Mirdif

This enormous shopping complex, a subsidize of the City Centre brand of malls by Majid Al Futtaim, is situated in the Mirdif, a family-friendly neighborhood. Some popular brands in this mall from where you can purchase your favorite products are Hamleys, Pottery Barn, Decathlon, Zara, Mango, and Crate & Barrell. In addition to shopping, City Centre Mirdif offers a wide range of other activities, such as Little Explorers, iFly Dubai (an innovative indoor skydiving activity), and Magic Planet.


Time – For Sunday to Wednesday, it's 10 am to 10 pm. For Thursday to Saturday, it's 10 am to 12 am.

Location –Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road – Mirdif
 

City Centre Deira

This is one of the oldest and most popular shopping malls in Dubai, and it's easy to see why. With over 400 stores, it has something for everyone. Like most City Centre malls, City Centre Deira mall incorporates a Magic Planet to occupy younger customers. For both visitors and locals, it is very accessible thanks to the Deira City Centre metro station. Like other malls, you will get a wide range of popular retail brands, cafes, and restaurants here. 

Time – For Sunday to Wednesday, it's 10 am to 10 pm. For Thursday to Saturday, it's 10 am to 12 am.

Location –8th Street – Port Saeed, Dubai

BurJuman Centre

This luxurious mall is home to high-end fashion brands, jewelers, and restaurants. If you're looking to treat yourself, BurJuman is the place to go.It is one of the perfect recreational areas in the city and is perfectly situated for inhabitants of the flats in Bur Dubai. This shopping center in Dubai is perfect for visiting with family and friends because it offers entertainment activities like Magic Planet, high street shops like Next, Nautica, and H&M, and luxury retailers like Louis Vuitton and Mont Blanc. 

Time – For Sunday to Wednesday, it's 10 am to 10 pm. For Thursday to Saturday, it's 10 am to 11 am.

Location –Khalid Bin Al Waleed Road – Al Mankhool, Dubai

Mercato Shopping Mall

Mercato shopping mall is one of Dubai's most beautiful commercial centers, and it literally means "market" in Italian. This mall, which boasts beautiful Renaissance-style architecture and a good assortment of shops, including Laura Ashley, Hallmark, and Mango, is particularly wonderful for mall trawls.

Time – Every day, 10 am to 10 pm.

Location –Jumeirah Beach Road, Jumeirah 1 – Dubai

Dubai Marina Mall

The mall is located on the waterfront of the Dubai Marina, with views of the Persian Gulf. The mall has a gross leasable area of 130,000 square meters (1,400,000 sqft). The mall is anchored by Carrefour, Geant hypermarket, Jashanmal National, and Indigo casualty.

The Dubai Marina Mall has a wide range of stores, including fashion, beauty, electronics, home furnishings, and more. The mall also has a food court, a variety of international cuisines, and a cinema. It is a great place to shop, dine, and relax. With its convenient location, wide range of stores, and various restaurants, the Dubai Marina Mall is a great choice for a shopping destination in Dubai.

Time – For Sunday to Wednesday, it's 10 am to 10 pm. For Thursday to Saturday, it's 10 am to 11 am.

Location –Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai Marina

Wafi Mall

This mall is themed around Ancient Egypt, and it's one of the unique shopping experiences in Dubai. It features an impressive food court with over 30 restaurants. Matalan, Marks & Spencer, and other well-known stores are in Wafi. Visitors can savor dining at Wafi Gourmet, Paul Cafe, Biella, and Bosporus Gourmet Restaurant.

 

Time – For Sunday to Wednesday, it's 10 am to 10 pm. For Thursday to Saturday, it's 10 am to 12 am.

Location –OudMetha Road, Dubai

Then you need to check out the top 10 shopping malls in the city! From the Mall of the Emirates to the Dubai Mall, these shopping hotspots offer everything you could possibly want – and more. So what are you waiting for? Start your shopping spree today!
