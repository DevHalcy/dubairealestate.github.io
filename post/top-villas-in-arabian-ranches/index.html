Top Villas In Arabian Ranches with High-End Features!

Emaar Arabian Ranches is the biggest community in Dubai with the best resort-like amenities & features. Here are some of the best villas developments in Emaar Arabian Ranches.

An exclusive lifestyle steeped in the desert’s mystery and beauty is waiting for you at Arabian Ranches. You will find here yourself drifting into a world where time moves at a leisurely pace. There are 4,000 themed villas available for sale in other developments, and peaceful neighborhoods and luxury lifestyles are waiting for you in this haven. So, here in this blog, we are elaborating on some of the best villa developments in the Emaar Arabian Ranches. But first of all, let’s explore the locations & facts of this community.

Highlights of Arabian Ranches

  • Spread over 6.5 million sq. ft
  • 15 full-size tennis courts
  • Sports & leisure amenities 
  • Many entry & exit points

Location

Emaar Arabian Ranches community (Phase 1, Phase 2, and Phase 3) is positioned in a peaceful region of Dubai, nearby Emirates Road. This road takes you directly to Abu Dhabi & Sharjah and Dubai International Airport is just 15 Minutes away from here. The Dubai Bypass Road is just a few minutes away from this community that will take you directly to RAK (Ras Al Khaimah) and Fujeirah. 

Top Villas In Arabian Ranches with High-End Features:-

As you know Arabian Ranches in Dubai is famous for luxury vills, but some villa developments deserve a separate mention in this blog. All these developments offer a wide range of freehold villas, that are perfect for your family, a vacation home, or an investment. So these are the best villas in Arabian Ranches. 

Casa Villas

Construction work for Casa Villas began in 2012 and was completed in 2015. Offering 253 comfortable Moroccan-style homes with 3, 4, and 5 Bedroom configurations. Every villa has a contemporary interior and exterior design, has a fully-fitted bathroom, built-in closet & kitchen, spacious terrace, parking space, and lawn. 

If we talk about the exterior of these villas then the facade is designed in a subtle sand color, and every unit has open landscaped areas around with the best leisure opportunities for residents. The project is completely sold out. 

Palmera Villas

These are large & modern villas with custom-finished interiors. The layout of these 3 & 4 Bed Villas is perfect for families with features like bright & open living room, maid room, large private garden and parking space, etc.  

Palmera villas in Arabian Ranches is a refined and well-equipped family-friendly development. Perfectly master planned with wide & well-maintained streets and a wide range of amenities. As a resident of Palmera, you will be able to walk next to the lake, visit the golf course and enjoy clubhouse amenities, enjoy shopping at the mall, or meet friends at the best dining outlets. 

Al Reem Villas

If you are looking for an affordable villa in Dubai then you must consider Arabian Ranches Al Reem. This development was launched in 2005 and completed in 2007, so definitely there is no fresh inventory left. But if any inventory is left in resale then you must acquire it as the highest ROI is guaranteed. Al Reem is the largest sub-community in Dubai and is divided into AL Reem 1, AL Reem 2, and AL Reem 3, offering 3 & 4 Bed Villas. This community comprises two lakes and many gardens, ideal for walking & jogging. Al Reem community also has its mosque, best amenities & features.

Saheel Villas

If you are looking for large villas with an expansive private garden then this is where your search ends. These one & two-story villas are available in 3, 4 & 5 Bed options in sizes starting from 3166 sq. ft. These expansive freehold villas are sold out but you can check inventory in the resale market. So, it is not possible to give an exact idea of the price of these villas. 

Just like other developments in Arabian Ranches, at Saheel Villas you will enjoy a wide range of common amenities like a kids' play area, creche, nursery, swimming pool, gym restaurants, mosque, clinics, etc. With proximity to the Arabian Ranches Golf Course, Saheel Villas’ residents enjoy the best views of the surroundings. 

Mirador Villas

This is one of the most popular freehold villas development in Arabian Ranches with 4 Bed, 5 Bed, 6 Bed & 7 Bed Options. If you are looking for Spanish-styled villas in Dubai then Mirador is the best choice for you. This is the only development in Dubai where you get actual European architecture at home. Arabian Ranches Golf Course is just adjacent to this development, which means you will get direct access to sports & eye-catching views from your home. These villas are sold out, so there is no update on the price & payment plan. 

June

This is one of the latest villas developed in Arabian Ranches by Emaar. A premium collection of twin villas with side gardens are available for sale. These branded villas are equipped with the best quality amenities & features for comfort and convenience. This development is centrally positioned inside the Emaar Arabian Ranches 3 Dubai with the best connectivity that presents easy and fast access to different parts of Dubai.  

Just like other developments in Arabian Ranches,  Emaar June is laced with the best facilities & features and are designed by reputed names in the industry, so you can have the best approach to living a luxurious life.

Conclusion

Arabian Ranches is one of the best & well-known family-friendly communities in Dubai. Launched in 3 phases (Arabian Ranches 1, Arabian Ranches 2, and Arabian Ranches 3), contains luxury villas & townhouses. Every phase of Arabian ranches has its dedicated amenities & facilities, and you enjoy the best connectivity from different parts of Dubai. Here neighborhoods are surrounded by green surroundings, parks, gardens, golf courses, outdoor sports facilities, and luxury amenities. Most of the units are sold out and few units are left. Whether you are a home buyer or real estate investor, this community presents you with exactly what you are looking for. 

As you know, Arabian Ranches Villas are almost sold out but if you are looking for a luxury freehold villa or other residential options in Dubai then explore Dubai Housing. Here you will get the best residential options from reputed developers at the best price.
