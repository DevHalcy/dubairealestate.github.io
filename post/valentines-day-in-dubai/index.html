Things To Do On Valentine's Day In Dubai

Valentine's Day is a special day to celebrate love and affection. And what better place to celebrate it than in Dubai - the city of luxury and romance.

There are numerous ways to celebrate Valentine's Day in Dubai. You can go on a romantic dinner date at one of the city's many fine dining restaurants. Or you can enjoy a special Valentine's Day spa treatment with your loved one.

For those who want to do something extra special, there are a number of Valentine's Day packages available at Dubai's top hotels. These packages typically include a luxurious room, champagne, flowers, and a special Valentine's Day breakfast in bed.

Let’s see what you can do with your special someone on this Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023. We will look at some of the best places to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Dubai along with some special activities that will make your day more memorable.

Burj Khalifa – The best place to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Dubai

Every people by now knows that Burj Khalifa is one of the hotspot of Dubai. Either its some special occasion or some seasonal festival, visiting this place is a must. Visit the BurjKhalifa's Observation Deck to get a bird's-eye perspective over Dubai and to experience what it's like to be at the top of the globe. Literally the best place to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Dubai. Sipping Arabian coffee with your special someone in the world's tallest building will undoubtedly be a memory you'll never forget. The beautiful coastline and cityscape of Dubai will provide unforgettable backdrops for your Valentine's Day celebration. A perfect plan to make your Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023 more memorable and luxurious.

Time: 8 AM - 3 PM; 3.30 PM - 6 PM

Location: Observation Deck, 1, Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Boulevard

IMG Worlds of Adventure – A best place for Valentine’s Day in Dubai

A best place for Valentine’s Day in Dubai if you want to satisfy the little kid inside you and experience the pure love with your special someone. IMF Worlds of Adventure is filled with some amazing rides for adults along with delicious food options. IMG Worlds Adventure will be embellished in heart-shaped décor from the 11th to the 14th of February so that guests may celebrate Valentine's Day while enjoying the park's thrilling attractions throughout its four distinct themed areas.

This Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023, give your special someone a memorable gateway at IMF Worlds of Adventure. You both can enjoy rides together. And after crazy and fun time together, you guys can have a cosy and romantic dinner at park. IMG Worlds Adventure also offers a wide range of romantic dinner options, specially for Valentine’s Day season. So, what are you waiting for? Book your tickets right away.

Time: 12 PM - 10 PM; 12 PM - 11 PM (Only for Saturday)

Location: E311 Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Rd, City of Arabia

Helicopter Ride – For a memorable Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023

Want to do something special for your partner in this Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023? Then what will be better than a helicopter ride! If you are not some who can enjoy a simple candle light dinner with your partner and want to do something unique and fun with your partner, then this is the best option! This Valentine’s Day in Dubai, get yourself inside the quiet cabin of a chopper and you'll see the City of Gold in ways you never thought possible.

See the Burj Al Arab, the Atlantis Hotel, the Meydan racetrack, and more from above. Right from departure, the flight's excitement and adrenaline will have your heart beating, making it a fantastic romantic excursion.Take advantage of this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity and create memories that will last a lifetime with an unforgettable Valentine's Day in Dubai 2023 for your special someone.

Time: Whenever you will book (maximum booking time is 40 mins)

Location: Nassimi Beach Parking, Atlantis Hotel, Palm Jumeirah

The Nara Desert Escape – Another best place for Valentine’s Day in Dubai

If you are finding the best place to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Dubai with some serenity and a holistic view, then the Nara Desert Escape is the one option for you. It is a vast desert camping place which has a wide range of service options, offering something for every people. Sand boarding, dune bashing, falcon displays, soft archery, camel rides, and many more intriguing desert attractions will guarantee that you will enjoy some quality and peaceful time with your special someone, and then you can top it off with some substantial delights.

Make your Valentine’s Day in Dubai more special and extra romantic by choosing the Nara Desert Escape as your romantic destination in Dubai. Witness the sky's transformation into a palette of colours as the sun sets, and then the sky comes alive with the twinkling of millions of stars. Savour the delicious desert feast that has been prepared for you.

Time: Whenever you will book (maximum for 5 to 6 hours)

Location: Al Ain - Dubai Rd

The La Perle Show – To make you Valentine’s Day in Dubai more special

If you and your partner are a tech-savvy people and would like to spend your Valentine’s Day in Dubai with some technological blast accompanied by art, then the La Perle Show would be the best place to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Dubai. If you and your significant other are the kind that enjoys going out to see a live show rather than a movie on Valentine's Day, or if you simply want to treat yourself to something really spectacular this year, the La Perle Show is your best chance.

This Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023, enjoy an incredible 90 minutes of acrobatic maneuvers, stunts, destruction dives and aquatic illusions, and some ingenious use of advanced technology is presented by world-class artistic performers. This nail-biting thriller will move you to tears and provide you with a wealth of fresh insights as it keeps you on the edge of your seat. These are the kinds of experiences that might become cherished memories for the two of you to look back on.

Time: two shows at 7 PM and 9:30 PM (Tuesday to Friday), on Saturday at 4 PM and 7 PM.

Location: Al Habtoor City

This Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023, experience a unique romantic dinner with your special someone by having dinner in the sky. As the breeze blows in your face, you'll get a little thrill with every mouthful. If you and your partner are the adventurous kind and want to push your relationship to new heights, then this is the article for you. While you eat, your security will be ensured and you'll be escorted by trained experts.

The Dubai Frame – Special place for a special Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023

Another best place to celebrate Valentine's Day in Dubai 2023. This Valentine's Day in Dubai, you could accompany your photography-obsessed significant other to Dubai Frame if they want to celebrate the holiday in style. You can take some stunning photographs at the world's largest picture frame, which is also home to some dazzling architecture.

It is the best place for Valentine’s Day in Dubai, specially if you a photoholic, as here you will be able to take the most romantic pictures with your loved ones. While you and your partner is the focus of attention within the enormous frame, you should keep in mind to create some sizzling positions. You may also visit the museum that is located within to find out how Dubai came to be known as the pinnacle of modernism and what the aspirations are for the city's future. Location: Zabeel Park Jogging Track,Za'abeelAlKifaf

Although Dubai is often thought of as a bustling metropolis, it also boasts some of the most luxurious and romantic resorts in the world. If you're looking to spoil your loved one this Valentine's Day in Dubai, consider spending a few days at one of Dubai's luxury resorts.

There are plenty of activities to keep you entertained at these resorts, from enjoying the sunny weather by the pool to indulging in a couples' massage at the spa. And of course, no romantic getaway is complete without some fine dining. Many of the resorts in Dubai offer world-class cuisine that is sure to impress your special someone which makes them the best place to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Dubai.

So if you're looking for a truly luxurious and romantic Valentine's Day in Dubai 2023, consider spending it at one of Dubai's luxury resorts. Your loved one is sure to appreciate the gesture, and you'll create memories that will last a lifetime.

Time: Depends on the location and your preferences

Location: A wide range to choose from such as Atlantis, the Palm; Bulgari Resort Dubai; Four Seasons Resort Dubai at Jumeirah Beach and many more.

The Miracle Garden – The best place to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Dubai for flower lovers

The best place for Valentine’s Day in Dubai if you are a nature and flower lover. A perfect place for an aesthetically impressive Valentine’s Day for this season. There is no greater symbol of love than flowers. Imagine surprise your special someone with a visit to a garden where there are 45,000,000 blooming flowers to admire. Stroll through the Miracle Garden's "heart path" and take in the breath-taking flora all around you.

The Miracle Garden debuted in 2013 on Valentine's Day, which literally makes it the best place to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Dubai. The biggest flower garden in the world is an aromatic and visual feast. It has stunning floral sculptures and a medley of enticing fragrances. A lengthy romantic stroll, hand in hand, across the flowering landscape is sure to evoke feelings and spark discussion, or at least provide a pleasant opportunity to stroll hand in hand.

Time: 9 AM - 9 PM, 9 AM - 11 PM (on Friday and Saturday)

Location: Al Barsha South 3, Barsha, Dubailand Area

Dhow Cruise – A best place for Valentine’s Day in Dubai

Want to experience luxury and serenity all together on this Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023? Then Dhow Cruise would be the best place to celebrate Valentine’s Day in Dubai. Enjoy a dinner date cruise around Dubai's picturesque Deira Creek on Valentine's Day in Dubai by booking a trip on a vintage Dhow Cruise. Outside, the lit-up ship sparkles like a mirage on the sea under the starry night sky; and inside, it's just as stunning.

A romantic meal on a classic Dhow in Dubai Creek is the epitome of serenity and opulence. Among the most romantic activities available in Dubai, this is a must-do. There are both cutting-edge and classic rides available, each with its own unique set of amenities (candlelit supper, soft music, special lighting, etc.).The cruise's mellow folk music, puppet and Tanoura displays, and general relaxing atmosphere are ideal for a quiet, intimate romance. Experience all these amazing activities on this Valentine’s Day in Dubai with your special someone.

Indulge in both contemporary and classical Arabic cuisine, then round up the night with a cocktail from the lounge. The romantically lit cityscape in the distance is the cherry on top.

Time: 8:30 PM - 10 PM

Location: Deira Creek

Enjoy a one-of-a-kind Valentine's Day celebration in Dubai with an activity that's perfect for active couples like you!

The Dubai Ice Rink is a great location to take a break from shopping, dining, and gawking at the Dubai Mall Fountain while also providing an opportunity to master the basics of ice skating. Plan a perfect romantic and icy Valentine’s Day in Dubai 2023 at Dubai Ice Rink.
