10 Unique Water Parks in Dubai - You Must Visit in 2023

2023 is here and what will be the best place to enjoy your New Year other than Dubai! Start off your New Year with a cool vibe by visiting best water parks in Dubai.

The amazing water parks in Dubai are becoming increasingly popular with tourists and locals alike. With their exciting rides and attractions, these parks are the perfect place to cool off 2022 and have a fun start for your 2023.

There are currently three best water parks in Dubai, and they are all worth a visit. The first is Wild Wadi, which is located on Jumeirah Beach. This park has a wave pool, several slides, and a river ride.

Next is Laguna Water Park, which is located in Dubai Marina. This park has a lazy river, a wave pool, and a number of slides.

Finally, there is YasWaterworld, which is located on Yas Island. This water park has a number of slides, a river ride, and a wave pool.

So, if you're looking for some fun in the sun, check out these 10 amazing water parks in Dubai!

Wild Wadi Water Park

Currently the best water park in Dubai is Wild Wadi. Whether you are coming with your family or friends or your partner, this place has to offer so much for every visitor. It includes almost 30 types of rides and various fun activities for kids as well as adults. For safety measurements, every place is secured with efficient guards. Your kids will be safe in this place all the time. Kid’s zone has been specifically prepared with extra safety and security.

Plunge into the chilly waters of the wave pools, speed down the winding slides, and get your heart racing on the heart-pounding attractions like BurjSurj and Tantrum Alley. It would be a perfect place if you want a break from your daily mundane life and start your 2023 with a refreshment. So, make sure to visit one of the best water parks in Dubai, Wild Wadi.

Location – Jumeirah Street, Next to Burj Al Arab, Jumeirah 2

Time – 10AM - 6PM

Laguna Water Park

Another one of the most famous water parks in Dubai to shake off your heat from 2022. Laguna Water Park is a great way to cool off during the summer. This place is great for people of any age and offer a variety of different attractions. This best water park in Dubai has slides that can be as high as 60 feet tall, wave pools, lazy rivers, and more. These attractions make it easy to keep cool in the hot weather.

Another main attraction of this water park is that it offers access to beautiful private beach where you can spend some quality time with your family and closed ones. A perfect gate away for your New Year celebration with a peaceful and quite vibe. Participate in activities that will get your heart racing, such as the Manta ride, which consists of sliding down an enclosed tube that winds through a sequence of turns and twists.

This amazing water park in Dubai will not disappoint you. The breath-taking panoramas of the Persian Gulf and the cityscape of Dubai can be enjoyed from the Laguna water park. Alternatively, you may sip a drink while lounging carefree in the warm rays of the sun.

Location – La mer, Dubai

Time – 10AM - 6PM

Yas Water Park

Do you love fantasy world? Then this water park in Dubai is the one for you! The unique snake body like architecture of this water park makes you feel like you are in a fantasy adventure, going through inside a body of a huge snake. One of the best water parks in Dubai saturated with wide range of rides and extremely fun activities.

This water park in Dubai is a genuinely fun-filled and exciting destination to spend your days, with wide range of 45 rides that are appropriate for people of all ages to enjoy. Enjoy the thrill of plunging into icy water from the peak of a hill and then slithering your way down into the pool while encased in a colossal snake.

Along with all these fun activities, you will also get a variety of food option in this park, start from Chinese cuisine to Thai and Indian cuisine. So, what are you waiting for, visit Yas Water Park now!

Location – Abu Dhabi

Time – 10AM - 6PM

Atlantis Aquaventure Water Park

Dubai Water Park Atlantis Aquaventure is one of the most biggest and popular water parks in the city you can witness. You can get all types of rides here. Either for kids or adults, Atlantis Aquaventure has everything to offer. The water park has some of the best heart-pounding and goosebump-inducing activities, such as bungie jumping and diving in lagoons that are full of sharks. Activities like these are available to guests.

Dubai’s biggest water park has so many things to offer you that one visit would not be enough for you. It has mesmerising private beaches which will be perfect for family, friends and couples. And if you want to give your children a special day by bringing them here, then Dubai’s biggest water park also has special ‘Splashers Children’s Play Area’. This special area for children is efficiently made with extra safety and security. So, leave all your worries at home and come to Atlantis Aquaventure Water Park to give your soul the real taste of fun!

Location – Atlantis The Palm, Crescent Rd

Time – 10AM - 6PM

Legoland Water Park

Legoland Water Park is a place where kids would want to go again and again to enjoy themselves. It is one of the best water parks in Dubai as a fun place for families, friends, and even groups of children to hang out. This water parks is a great place for people to have fun and cool off during the summer. They provide entertainment for all ages. There are many different types of activities included in this water park in Dubai but some of its best features are slides, pools, lazy rivers, and wave pools.

It is the best water park in Dubai for kids who are aged between 2 to 12. It is a wonderful place for you and your family to spend the day outside having fun. You will get the impression that you've been taken to a film set the moment you step foot in this park since the whole place is created in the manner of Legos. This water park in Dubai has more than 20 rides for kids which are fun and safe.

Location – Opposite Palm, Sheikh Zayed Rd

Time – 1AM - 6PM (Sunday to Wednesday), 11AM - 7PM (Thursday and Saturday), 11AM - 8PM (Friday)

IMG Worlds of Adventure

It is one of the famous and largest water parks in Dubai. There are many services provided by IMG Worlds of Adventure. One of them is the water slides and other rides. You will be able to find more than 20 types of slides and rides in huge water park in Dubai.The whole water park is subdivided into four separate giant entertainment zones, each of which has a unique collection of attractions based on a distinct theme, such as cartoon network characters, Marvel superheroes, as well as dinosaurs.

The second service that this water park provides is the food. A lot of people visit a water park solely for the food options available to them. Well, in this one of the best water parks in Dubai, you will get many types of food available starting from burgers to pizza and even ice cream!

The third service that this water park provides is the lifeguards. The lifeguards are there for everyone's safety, so they make sure that no one goes down any ride without someone else accompanying them at all times. They also make sure that everyone has enough space on the slides and rides.

Location – E311 Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Rd

Time - 12PM - 9PM

Dreamland Aqua Park

It is one of the newest Dubai’s biggest water park. Visit Dubai's Dreamland Aqua Park along with your loved ones to enjoy an exciting day out in the city, and prepare to be swept away into a wondrous world full of happiness and unexpected pleasures.Anyone's senses will be revived by the combination of the park's verdant setting and vibrant rides and attractions. If you want a break from your daily busy life, then this water park in Dubai would be a perfect destination for you. It will help you to rejuvenate your soul and mind. It would also be a perfect place for your kids to have fun.

Dreamland Aqua Parkis a great place to spend a vacation thanks to its 2,50,000 square metres of attractions and activities, including water slides, coasters, a tennis court, a large wave pool, a movie zone, and camping.

Location – Umm Al Quwain

Time – 10AM - 6PM

Splash n Party

It is a bestwater park in Dubai for kids to have unlimited fun time. You will have enough time to appreciate the sophisticated restaurant and the lively juice bar that are both located inside the complex since it has a broad variety of attractions including water slides and splash pads that are meant to keep the children occupied.

Kids will be able to have unlimited enjoyment time and there will be plenty of options for drinks and food. This famous water park in Dubai has everything to offer them. Staring from wide range of fun activities to utmost security for their safety. This New Year, Splash n Party would be the favourite water park for children.

You also have the option of throwing your children a birthday party here that will be one of the most memorable events in their whole life if you choose to hold it here. Check out their catering services for special occasions held in the comfort of your own home as well.

Location – Al Safa 2, Street 8A, Villa no.1, Jumeirah

Time – 9AM - 8PM

AquaFun

AquaFun, which is renowned for being the largest inflatable water park in Dubai, is an absolute must-visit location for anybody who is searching for a one-of-a-kind adventure that anyone will treasure for the rest of their lives.

There are many reasons why AquaFun is popular and one of the best water parks in Dubai. First of all, this water park provides the opportunity to spend time in the company of others and have fun while staying cool in the hot summer heat. Secondly, it offers many activities such as slides, lazy rivers, and wave pools which can be enjoyed by people of all ages.

So, don’t think much and take your kids to this water park in Dubai to give them a best time and also give yourself a break from your daily mundane life.

Location – The Beach, opposite to JBR

Time – 10M - 7PM

MotionGate

If you are a fan of Hollywood culture and are looking for a way to spend a pleasant day in Dubai with your family, MotionGate could be the best option for you. MotionGate is a major theme water park in Dubai that is designed after Hollywood and offers a variety of themed areas with a multitude of attractions that are not only a treat to visit but are also a pleasure to explore.

You'll find everything from roller coaster rides to slides that are filled with activities to offer you the ultimate dosage of thrills, as well as a variety of different live shows and cuisine selections that are designed to pamper you. So, what are you waiting for? Let’s visit to one of the best water parks in Dubai and start your New Year with a cool Hollywood vibe.

Location – Park and Resorts, Sheikh Zayed Road

Time – 2PM - 10PM
