What Is An Apartment?

The basic definition of an apartment or a flat is that it is planned on a large land parcel where the builder brings a collection of apartments to bring like minded families together and serve a high range of modern amenities in the vicinity.

Usually, it is an area crammed with 1, 2, 3, 4 or more bedrooms along with a bathroom, kitchen, dining area and balcony, so a family with 2 to 7 members can easily reside in with complete privacy and comfort.

Do You Want To Know From Where The Word Apartment Came?

Actually, the apartment comes from the French word where the meaning “A Separated Place” and on the funnier side, a single building comes with multi apartments and they are kind of stuck together when seen from the outer facade.

And there are common areas in society such as green sitting space, car parking zone, community hall, jogging track, and play area that brings families together to get into different facilities and socialize way better.

And the average lifespan of any concrete structure is 75-100 years where which ensures 100 percent safety for the owners.  

Should I Rent Or Own An Apartment?

Even in general it is said that buying is always better if you can and definitely there are factors that impact your decision where you need to be clear about the purpose of the property investment or personal use.

So if you think things are more transient, then rental is the best option, and if you know you are going to stay longer, then purchasing is the lucrative choice.

Rental Yield in Dubai range from 5-10%, so it might be a little expensive, rather buying is a great option because too many units are available that fit in the budget smoothly and even it promises you better ROI in the future.

So all in all, rent or buying both depends on the duration of your stay and current funds.

Pros Of Living In An Apartment

  • 100% Access to the Space

Better access to the space where apartments leave no space vacant and the resident gets its far better using all the time.

  • Improved Security 

Generally, apartment housing comes with 5 tier security system so this controls the surroundings way better and even if you leave your kids at home they have a secure atmosphere to stay in and play. And even for elders, it creates an umbrella of comfort.

  • Complete Community 

The biggest advantage to consider while living in an apartment is that it provides wider access to a high range of modern amenities- gym, spa, clubhouse, sports hub, swimming pool, and lots more.

Other than this, a community brings like-minded families together where it has a better platform to socialize.

  • On your Demand

For the maintenance part when you need a plumber, gardener, electrician, or anyone they are available on your demand, so you don’t have to look up for them or arrange from outside, rather on your demand they are within the community.

  • Wide range of options

When it comes to selection you have a wider range of options with you where you can opt for the right choice depending on your budget & need.

 As you know there are two sides of the same coin, so there are cons too of buying an apartment, so let us list down for you:-

Cons Of Living In An Apartment

  • Privacy and Noise

On a single floor there are maximum 4 apts which might share a wall, so this way it disturbs your privacy and even creates noise, so these distractions you need to consider before buying an apartment.

  • Rules and Regulations

If you plan to rent your apartment then you need to follow many rules & regulations, so you need to follow codes of conduct before you plan to buy an apartment.

  • Needs Permission

For the inner construction and changes you need to take permission from the management team and give them a letter to take consent and also you are not allowed to change the facade of the building, so you have to keep it as it is.
