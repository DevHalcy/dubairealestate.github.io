This guide will provide you with all the information you need to make your New Year in Dubai one to remember. We’ll cover what to expect in Dubai for New Year’s Eve, different places to celebrate New Year in Dubai, and where to stay. So, let’s get started!

What to Expect in Dubai for New Year Eve?

It’s no secret that Dubai is a party city and New Year’s Eve is no exception. Every year, the city comes alive with extravagant celebrations to mark the end of the year. Dubai is well-known for its dazzling firework displays, which illuminate the city skyline and can be seen from all over the city.

For those looking for an even more spectacular celebration, there are plenty of luxurious rooftop parties and events to choose from. These are held at some of the city’s most iconic venues. To help you make the best of your New Year experience in Dubai, here are some of the best places to celebrate New Year in Dubai for 2023.

Top 9 Places to Celebrate New Year in Dubai

Aura Skypool Lounge

Aura Skypool Lounge is a rooftop lounge in Dubai, located on the 30th floor of the 5-star Address Sky View Hotel. It is known for its chic and modern ambiance, with stunning views of the Dubai skyline. With a sophisticated pool, stunningly designed cabanas, and a live DJ, Aura Skypool Lounge is an excellent spot to relax and enjoy the city’s stunning views. The lounge also offers a range of food and drinks from its award-winning restaurant and bar, as well as an extensive selection of cigars. It's a unique setting, Aura Skypool Lounge is the ideal spot to enjoy a night out in Dubai. With an outdoor swimming pool, barbeque area, and comfortable seating, Aura Skypool Lounge is the perfect place to celebrate New Year in Dubai.

Casa De Tapas

Casa De Tapas is known as a place to enjoy a unique Spanish dining experience.

Located in The Pointe at Palm Jumeirah, Casa de Tapas boasts stunning panoramic views of the Arabian Gulf. As you celebrate the New Year, enjoy a delicious selection of authentic Spanish tapas and paella. With a lively atmosphere and a wide selection of drinks, Casa de Tapas is a fabulous location to celebrate New Year 2023 in Dubai.

Eataly

Eataly is a great option for those looking for an exclusive dining experience this New Year.

Located in the Mall of the Emirates, Eataly is a modern Italian food market that offers an incredible selection of fresh, seasonal ingredients and delicious dishes. Enjoy a delicious Italian feast with your family and friends as you usher in the New Year.

Hells Kitchen

Hell’s Kitchen is an underground nightclub featuring live DJs, international acts and the best in hip hop, funk, soul and house music.

With an expansive dance floor and some of the best sound and lighting systems in the city, Hell’s Kitchen is sure to have you dancing the night away into the New Year. For those looking to take their celebration up a notch, you can reserve a private booth or table for the night and enjoy a bottle service experience. With VIP packages available, you can also enjoy exclusive access to the VIP lounge and a dedicated team of wait staff and bartenders. 

Nikki Beach

Located on the Palm Jumeirah, Nikki Beach features a stunning beachfront location and offers an array of fine dining experiences. Enjoy a delicious seafood feast as you take in the incredible views of the Arabian Gulf. With a live DJ and a vibrant atmosphere, Nikki Beach is the superb spot to celebrate the New Year in Dubai.

Tamoka And Cana By Tamoka

Tamoka and Cana by Tamoka is the finest place for a unique New Year celebration.

This chic restaurant and lounge offers a unique dining experience with its traditional Lebanese cuisine and live music performances. With a wide selection of delicious dishes and drinks, Tamoka and Cana by Tamoka is a great place to spend New Year 2023 in Dubai.

The Tap House

New Year's night at The Tap House in Dubai is a night to remember!

The lively atmosphere is filled with music, drinks and laughter. As the clock strikes midnight, the bar erupts with cheers and the air is filled with confetti. After that, the party continues with an array of food and drinks, including special craft beers, shots and cocktails. Throughout the night, guests can enjoy live music and DJs, as well as fun activities like karaoke and pool. 

Traiteur

Traiteur is the place to enjoy a luxurious New Year celebration.

Located in the Park Hyatt Hotel, Traiteur offers an exquisite selection of French cuisine. Enjoy a delicious dinner with your family and friends as you take in the stunning views of the Dubai skyline. With an outdoor terrace, live DJ, and a vibrant atmosphere, Traiteur is the exquisite place to spend New Year 2023 in Dubai.
 

TJs

TJ's in Dubai is a popular spot for New Years celebrations.

Every year they host a grand event with live music, fireworks, and an amazing atmosphere. The decorations are nothing short of spectacular and the food is even better. Guests can choose from a variety of delicious dishes, from international cuisine to local delicacies. There are also plenty of drinks to choose from, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. The party is always a blast, and the view of the fireworks from the rooftop is unbeatable.
 

Conclusion

No matter what kind of experience you’re looking for, there are plenty of amazing places to celebrate New Year in Dubai.
