Enjoy Luxury With Tradition!

Hajar 2 Villas are made using a combination of ancient materials and modern design techniques. The stone construction is strong, durable, and visually appealing, and it also helps to regulate the temperature inside the villas. In addition to their attractive appearance, the villas are also energy-efficient and environmentally friendly.

Hajar 2 is the second phase of the Hajar villa project in Akoya Oxygen (Damac Hills 2). It offers 3 and 4 bedroom eco-friendly units with modern features & amenities. These villas are designed to blend in with their natural surroundings. They have large windows and private gardens and terraces, allowing residents to enjoy nature from inside the villas. These Villas are a luxurious yet low-maintenance housing option for those looking to invest in a new home.

Price

Hajar 2 Villas are available in a range of sizes and configurations with the best features & facilities at a very attractive price.

Amenities

Residents of Hajar 2 villas have access to every amenity & facility of Damac Hills 2, including nature-inspired amenities, gourmet dining options, retail boutiques, and an international golf club.
